15. (With solution)The combined mass of a Bulldog and an angora cat is 48 kg. Bulldog's mass is seven times heavy as the Angora cat. Find the mass of each animal.​


Let x= the mass of the Angora cat and

7x= the mass of the Bulldog

mathematical sentence:

[tex]x + 7x = 48[/tex]

solution:

Find x to get the mass of the cat,

[tex]x + 7x = 48 \\ 8x = 48 \\ \frac{8x}{8} = \frac{48}{8} \\ x = 6[/tex]

Using x=6, find 7x to get the mass of the dog,

[tex]7x \\ = 7(6) \\ = 42[/tex]

checking:

x=6 and 7x=42,

[tex]x + 7x = 48 \\ \\ 6 + 42 = 48 \\ 48 = 48[/tex]

conclusion:

Therefore, the mass of the Angora cat is 6kg and the mass of the Bulldog is 42kg.
