25. a brick wall has 60 bricks in first row , but each row has 3 fewer bricks than the previous one. how many bricks are in the 12th row?


Answer:

There are 27 bricks in the 12th row

Step-by-step explanation:

Arithmetic Sequence:

An = a₁ + (n-1)(d)

Where:

First term, a₁ = 60

Common difference, d = -3 (decreasing number of bricks; keyword: fewer)

Last row/terms, n = 12

a₁₂ = 60 + (12-1)(-3)

a₁₂ = 60 + (11)(-3)

a₁₂ = 60 - 33

a₁₂ = 27

There are 27 bricks in the 12th row.
