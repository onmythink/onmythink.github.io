22. Decopage painting is where canvases are padded, sewn, and often filled with sequins, beads, shells, buttons, tiny mirrors, glass, swatches and other things to make an artistic composition. Select one: True False


Answer

False

This is not Decoupage, it is called Trapunto.

Explanation

Decoupage

It is a method of decorating an object by gluing colored cutout designs unto the surface of the object. Each cutout is sealed by a layer of varnish until the cutouts look like a painting or inlay. The traditional technique uses 30 - 40 layers of varnish then, finished by sanding to have a polished appearance.

Trapunto

It is an Italian word that means "to quilt" in English. Trapunto is a quilting method that stuffs the decorative materials to produce a puffy artistic design. It requires at least two layers of canvas or textile, the first layer being the padded layer creating the raised surface of the quilt.
