5. CC Stands for Select one:a. Canary Copyb. Carrier Copyc. Courier Copyd. Carbon Copy​


CC Stands for Carbon Copy.

CC is a feature of an e-mail, wherein recipients under this fields can receive the message but they are not required to send an acknowledgement or reply to the sender. They are in the list to receive the email because they must be informed. Their Departments must have a concern on the email subject and conversation but they are not required to send their replies in the thread or conversation.  

CC has another form, which is BCC. BCC stands for Blank Carbon Copy, wherein, recipients can receive the email but other recipients cannot see their email addresses for confidentiality purposes.

10. Read the sentence. Pet canaries need large cages, wooden perches, some fresh water, and keep out of cold. Which phrase should be revised for a more parallel sentence? large cages wooden perches some fresh water keep out of cold


If we want to make a parallel sentence, then you must be aware of the terms used. Make sure that the terms used are related to each other and their idea belong to the same group or category. In this statement, large cages, wooden perches, and some fresh water are all physical tangible things that we can provide. On the other hand, keep out of cold is more of an action rather than something that is given.  

So, the answer is:

D. keep out of cold

20. The Canary Islands in the Pacific are named after what animal?


Answer:

The Canary Islands are not located in the Pacific, but rather in the Atlantic Ocean, near the coast of Africa. They are named after dogs, not canaries. The name "Canary" comes from the Latin word "Canariae Insulae", meaning "Islands of the Dogs".

Answer:

The Canary Islands are actually located in the Atlantic Ocean, near the coast of Africa. They are named after dogs, not canaries. The Latin name for dog is "canis," and the islands were named "Canariae Insulae" by the Romans because of the large number of wild dogs they found there.
