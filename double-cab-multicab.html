1. a multicab racing 1000 kg is moving at the speed of 5m what is the kinetic energy of the multicab​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The kinetic energy of a moving object is given by

[tex]\boxed{KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^{2}}[/tex]

where:

KE = kinetic energy

m = mass

v = speed

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the kinetic energy

[tex]KE = \dfrac{1}{2}mv^{2}[/tex]

[tex]KE = \dfrac{1}{2}(\text{1,000 kg})(\text{5 m/s})^{2}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{KE = \text{12,500 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the kinetic energy of the multicab is 12,500 J.

15. A multicab starter motor has a current of 60.0 a and a voltage of 12v what is the resistance of the starter motor


Formula:

Ohm's law

[tex]\boxed{V=IR}[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]R=\dfrac{V}{I} =\dfrac{12\text{ V}}{60.0\text{ A}}=\boxed{0.20\:\Omega}[/tex]

The resistance of the starter motor is 0.20 Ω.
