3. looking good online means having a good profile pictures? true or false?​


The answer is False.

Looking good online is not literally using a nice profile picture for yourself in social media and websites. Looking good online simply means, practicing good netiquette or practicing acceptable behavior when you are online or connected to the internet. This includes responsible use of social media and other internet resources. You can be considered a good internet user if you know how to practice good netiquettes.

Here are some examples of good netiquettes: Refrain from typing in all capital letters or caps lock when you are talking to someone online. This is considered disrespectful and shouting. Avoid posting unrelated replies to other posts or threads. This could be mistaken as trolling or spamming. Trolling is an act of provoking somebody to fight in a thread. Spamming is posting or sending unsolicited messages, over and over again. Do not support or do cyberbullying. Cyberbullying is an act of bullying online. This is common in social media. Act as you are in real life. Ensure that how you act in the virtual world or online is also how you will act and take the situation on real life. Be respectful to adults, especially to your teachers and elders.

Agri News

zbsarian January 14, 2019

VICENTE LUGAGAY HOLDING A TILAPIA BREEDER

A 53-year-old former ordinary farm worker in a fish farm quit his job and put up his own tilapia hatchery in Brgy. Rizal, Santiago City in Isabela. In 2018, he was the Gawad Saka winner in the Fish Culture Category and received P300,000 in prizes from the national and regional levels. Of course, that’s peanuts compared to his income from the sales of his tilapia fingerlings.

Vicente Lugagay produces 300,000 to 500,000 fingerlings a month which he sells not only in Isabela but also in other places in Nueva Vizcaya, Cagayan, Pampanga and the Cordillera Administrave Region.

Lugagay won his national and regional awards because of the practical and innovative technologies he has adopted in running his tilapia hatchery

CONTRIBUTIONS:

MORE STURDY FINGERLINGS – Lugagay also installed a special pump in his conditioning tanks instead of the conventional flow-through and aeration system. This allows for more sturdy fingerlings, thereby higher survival. Sex reversal is achieved in only four days instead of the usual 21 days.
