8. Rhombus LMNO is shown with its diagonals. Angle MLO measures 112°. What is the measure of angle MLP? °


Answer:

56°

Step-by-step explanation:

One of the properties of the diagonals of a Rhombus is they bisect each other. This means that they cut each other in half.

Thus, the angle of MLP is the half of MLO.  

Since the angle of MLO = 112° ,  

Therefore,  

∠ MLP = 112° / 2

 = 56 °

Therefore the angle of MLP is 56°.
