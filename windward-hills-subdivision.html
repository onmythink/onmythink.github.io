5. give plant that can live in windward​


Plants that can live in windward areas need to be able to tolerate strong winds and salt spray. Here are a few examples of plants that can thrive in these conditions:

1. Sea oats (Uniola paniculata) - a tall grass that grows in sand dunes and can tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

2. Beach morning glory (Ipomoea pes-caprae) - a vine that grows on sandy beaches and can tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

3. Seagrape (Coccoloba uvifera) - a tree that grows in coastal areas and can tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

4. Saltbush (Atriplex spp.) - a shrub that grows in coastal areas and can tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

5. Beach sunflower (Helianthus debilis) - a low-growing plant that grows on sandy beaches and can tolerate salt spray and strong winds.

It's important to note that different regions may have different native plant species that are adapted to the specific windward conditions of that area.

28. which side of the mountain is the windward side


Answer:

The side of the mountain that faces the wind is known as the windward side. This side often receives warm and moist air from the ocean.

Further Explanation:

1. There are two sides of the mountain; the leeward side and the windward side. The leeward side is the dry side and the windward side is the moist part.

2. Leeward side is the side that is sheltered or protected from the prevailing wind.

3. The windward side is the colder and the wetter side. This side has moist air travelling upward that will begin to condense at some point then later on becomes rain.

Keywords: Windward, Leeward
