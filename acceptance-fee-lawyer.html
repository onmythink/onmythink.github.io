16. Mary Hall’s will, specifies that her lawyer is to liquidate her assets and divide the proceeds among her three sisters. Lena’s share is to be one-half of Lisa’s, and Lisa’s share is to be one-half of Lauren’s. If the lawyer has agreed to a fee that is equal to 10% of the largest share and the proceeds amount to $164,428, then how much does each person get?


Answer:

Amount of each person gets:

Lena - $22,220Lisa - $44,440Lauren - $88,880Lawyer - $8,880

Step-by-step explanation:

Money Problem

Money word problems are the problems involving money. They involve decimal, fraction, discount or interest.

Let us analyze the given problem above.

What is asked?

The amount of each person gets.

What are the given?

Let x be the smallest amount which is the lawyer's fee.

lawyer's fee - 10% of the largest share = x

amount of Lauren's share = 10x

amount of Lisa’s share - ½ of Lauren’s = ½ (10x)

amount of Lena’s share - ½ of Lisa’s = {½ [½ (10x)]}

total amount of proceeds - $164,428

There are four steps to find the amount of each person gets:

Find the amount of lawyer's fee.Find the amount of Lauren's share.Find the amount of Lisa's share.Find the amount of Lena's share.

What is the equation?

x + 10x + ½ (10x) + {½ [½ (10x)]} = $164,428

Solution:

Step #1

Solve using PEMDAS.

x + 10x + ½ (10x) + {½ [½ (10x)]} = $164,428

x + 10x + 5x + ½ (5x) = $164,428

x + 10x + 5x + 2.5x = $164,428

18.5x = $164,428

18.5x/18.5 = $164,428/18.5

x = $8,888

Lawyer's fee is $8,888.

Step #2

amount of Lauren's share

= 10x = 10 × $8,888 = $88,880

Lauren's share is $88,880.

Step #3

amount of Lisa’s share

= ½ of Lauren’s = ½ ($88,880) = $44,440

Lisa's share is $44,440.

Step #4

amount of Lena’s share

= ½ of Lisa’s = ½ ($44,440) = $22,220

Lena's share is $22,220.

Final Answer:

Lena - $22,220

Lisa - $44,440

Lauren - $88,880

Lawyer - $8,880

Checking:

Lena + Lisa + Lauren + Lawyer = $164,428

$22,220 + $44,440 + $88,880 + $8,880 = $164,428

$164,428 = $164,428 ✔
