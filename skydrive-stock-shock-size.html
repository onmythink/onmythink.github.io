30. A store sells poster board. The table shows the colors and sizes in-stock. Which of the following metric approximations is the same as the measures of the green poster board?​


MEASUREMENT

[tex]__________________________[/tex]

As stated in the question, we have to find the approximation of the green poster board. In the given we can see that the measurement of the green poster is 11 in x 14 in.

Convert 11 in x 14 in to cm

[tex]\sf 11 \: \cancel{in} \: ( \frac{2.54 \: cm}{1 \: \cancel{in}} ) = 27.94 \: cm \: ≈28 \: cm[/tex]

[tex]\sf 14 \: \cancel{in} \: ( \frac{2.54 \: cm}{1 \: \cancel{in}} ) = 35.56 \: cm \: ≈36 \: cm[/tex]

↬ Hence, the same measurement of 11 in by 14 in is C. 28 cm by 36 cm
