23. tulong po help is appreiciated..Tom's,Bob's and Fred's house are in a triangle Tom's house is 7ft away from Bob's house. Fred's house is 5ft away from Tom's house. What is the perimeter of the triangle between their houses​


Answer:

Triangle perimeter calculation.

To find the perimeter of the triangle between their houses, we need to know the distance between Bob's house and Fred's house. Let's use the Pythagorean theorem to find it:

a^2 + b^2 = c^2

Where c is the distance between Bob's house and Fred's house, a is the distance between Tom's house and Bob's house (which we know is 7ft), and b is the distance between Tom's house and Fred's house (which we know is 5ft).

So:

7^2 + 5^2 = c^2

49 + 25 = c^2

74 = c^2

c = sqrt(74)

c ≈ 8.6ft

Now we can find the perimeter of the triangle by adding up the three sides:

7ft + 8.6ft + 5ft = 20.6ft

Therefore, the perimeter of the triangle between their houses is approximately 20.6 feet.

30. 1. Lam-Ang from the famous literary work "Biagni Lam-Ang​


Answer:

Pedro Bukaneg (March 1592 – c. 1630) was a Filipino poet. Blind since birth, he is the acknowledged author of the Ilocano epic Biag ni Lam-ang (Life of Lam-ang). He is considered the "Father of Ilocano literature." A street inside the Cultural Center of the Philippines (CCP) complex in Pasay City, Philippines is named in his honor. His surname is lent to the Bukanegan, the Ilocano equivalent of the Balagtasan.
