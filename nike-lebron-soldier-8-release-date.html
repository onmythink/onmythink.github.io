14. sex years ago, romel was five times as old as lebron. in five years, romel will be three times as old as lebron. what is the present age of lebron?


Answer:

Present age of Lebron is 17 years.


Step-by-step explanation:

Let, six years ago,

Age of Lebron was a years,

And, age of Romel was 5 times of lebron's age( that is 5a years ).


So,

⇒ Age of Lebron before 6 years = a years

⇒ Age of Romel before 6 years = 5a years


Now,

⇒ Present age of Lebron = a + 6 years

⇒ Present age of Romel = 5a + 6 years


After 5 years,

⇒ Age of Lebron = a + 6 + 5 years  

                             = ( a + 11 ) years

⇒ Age of Romel  = 5a + 6 + 5 years

                            = ( 5a + 11 ) years


According to the question : -

In five years, romel will be three times as old as lebron.

Hence,

⇒ Age of Romel after 5 years = 3 times the age of Lebron after 5 years

= >  ( 5a + 11 ) years = 3 x ( a + 11 ) years

= >  5a + 11 = 3( a + 11 )

= >  5a + 11 = 3a + 33

= >  5a - 3a = 33 - 11

= >  2a = 22

= >  a = 22 / 2

= >  a = 11


So,

Present age of Lebron = a + 6 years = ( 11 + 6 ) years = 17 years
