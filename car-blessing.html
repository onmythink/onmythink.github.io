12. a force of 100 and was applied to a toy car with a mass of 1 kg. what is the acceleration of the toy car?please pa sagot po need na need ko po to. thankyouuu in advance and god bless you!!​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The acceleration of an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{a = \frac{F}{m}}[/tex]

where:

a = acceleration

F = force

m = mass

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the acceleration

[tex]a = \dfrac{F}{m}[/tex]

[tex]a = \dfrac{\text{100 N}}{\text{1 kg}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{a = \text{100 m/}\text{s}^{2}}[/tex]

Therefore, the acceleration of the object is 100 m/s².
