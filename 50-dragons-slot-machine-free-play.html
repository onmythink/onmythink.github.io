3. A gambler begins playing a slot machine with $10 in quarters in her coin bucket. She plays 15 quarters before winning a jackpot of 50 quarters. She then plays 20 more quarters in the same machine before walking away. How many quarters does she now have in her coin bucket?​


Answer:

55 quarters.

Step-by-step explanation:

$10 is 40 quarters. (10 divided by 1/4 is 40.)

The gambler played 15 quarters, which means he's left with 25 quarters. (40-15 = 25).

Then, he won 50 quarters, having an accumulated quarter of 75 (25 + 50 = 75).

Lastly, he played for 20 more quarters, then stopped. He must have 55 quarters left. (75 - 20 = 55)

Mathematical Sequence:

1.) 10 / (1/4) = 10 x 4 = 40

2.) 40 - 15 = 25

3.) 25 + 50 = 75

4.) 75 - 20 = 55
