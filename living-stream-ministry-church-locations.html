10. what are the various roles and ministries we have in the church? how do they contribute to the building up of the church?​


AnswerWhat are the various roles and ministries we have in the church?

Various roles and ministries in a church are vast. In 1 Corinthians 12: 4-11, Paul simplified this into Gifts of the Holy Spirit. These are gifts of Wisdom, Knowledge, Faith, Healing, Miracles, Prophecy, Discernment, Speaking in Tongues (different languages), Spiritual Teaching.

The ministry of an individual Christian will depend on what gift he received from the Holy Spirit.

How do they contribute to the building up of the church?​

In 1 Corinthians 12: 12-27, Paul describes the church as the Body of Christ. The Church(Body) is made of different people(organ). These "organs" have their assigned role in the "Body". 1 Corinthians 12:25 says "In that way, the parts of the body will not take sides. All of them will take care of one another". So ministries contribute to the building up of the church by helping and support each other. If one "organ" fails the rest are affected.
