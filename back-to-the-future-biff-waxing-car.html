4. Ronald can wash and wax one car in 3 hours. Marco can do the Ronald can wash and wax one car in 3 hours. Marco can do the same job in 4 hours. If they worked together, how long will it take them to wash and wax the car? same job in 4 hours. If they worked together, how long will it take them to wash and wax the car? Show your complete solution


Answer:

12/7 hours or 1.71 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's first find out how much of the job Ronald can do in one hour:

Ronald can wash and wax one car in 3 hours, so in one hour he can do 1/3 of the job.

Similarly, Marco can do the same job in 4 hours, so in one hour he can do 1/4 of the job.

If they work together, we can add up their individual rates to find their combined rate:

1/3 + 1/4 = 7/12

So together, Ronald and Marco can do 7/12 of the job in one hour.

To find how long it will take them to complete the entire job, we can use the formula:

time = work / rate

Since they need to do the entire job, the work they need to do is 1.

Plugging in the rate we found, we get:

time = 1 / (7/12) = 12/7

Therefore, it will take them 12/7 hours, or approximately 1.71 hours, to wash and wax the car if they work together.
