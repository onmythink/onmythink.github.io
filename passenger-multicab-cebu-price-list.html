5. a multicab racing 1000 kg is moving at the speed of 5m what is the kinetic energy of the multicab​


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The kinetic energy of a moving object is given by

[tex]\boxed{KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^{2}}[/tex]

where:

KE = kinetic energy

m = mass

v = speed

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the kinetic energy

[tex]KE = \dfrac{1}{2}mv^{2}[/tex]

[tex]KE = \dfrac{1}{2}(\text{1,000 kg})(\text{5 m/s})^{2}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{KE = \text{12,500 J}}[/tex]

Therefore, the kinetic energy of the multicab is 12,500 J.

Answer:

80 passengers

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x & y be the no. of passengers from Manila to Cebu and Manila to Davao respectively.

Eq. 1: 1800x + 3500y = 494000

Eq. 2: x + y = 180

We need to determine the number of passengers that got off the plane in Cebu (x).

By substitution,

1800x + 3500(180 - x) = 494000

1800x + 630000 - 3500x = 494000

136000 = 1700x

=> x = 80, it follows that y = 100.

Thus, 80 passengers got off the plane in Cebu.

20. an airline flies from manila to davao with a stopover in cebu.it charges a fare of 1,800 pesos manila to cebu and a fare of 3,500 pesosfrom manila to davao.in one flight a total of 180 passengers boarded the plane in manila and the collected fare totalled 494,000 pesos. how many passengers got off the plane in cebu?​


Answer:

80 passengers got off the plane in Cebu.

Step-by-step explanation:

Passengers:

To Cebu: x

To Davao: y

Total passengers: 180

Equation (1):

x + y = 180

y = 180 - x  (passengers to Davao in terms of x)

Total fare:

Manila to Cebu: 1,800x

Manila to Davao: 3,500y

Total passenger fare for both destinations:  494,000

Equation (2):

1,800x + 3,500y = 494,000

Substitute 180-x to y in Equation (2):

1,800x + 3,500y = 494,000

1,800x + 3,500(180-x) = 494,000

Find the number of passengers to Cebu, x:

1,800x -3,500x + 630,000 = 494,000

-1700x = 494,000 - 630,000

-1700x/-1,700 = -136,000/-1700

x = 80 passengers to Cebu

Number of passengers to Davao, y:

y = 180-x

y = 180 - 80

y = 100 passengers to Davao

Total passenger fare for each destination:

Manila to Cebu:

1,800x = 1,800(80) = 144,000 pesos

Manila to Davao:

3,500y = 3,500(100) = 350,000 pesos

Check:

Total passengers:

80 + 100 = 180

180 = 180  (True)

Total fare:

144,000 + 350,000 = 494,000

494,000 = 494,000 (True)
