4. what type of boundary happened in canyon?​


Answer:

Transform boundary.

Explanation:

What type of boundary forms a canyon?

Natural or human-made structures that cross a transform boundary are offset — split into pieces and carried in opposite directions. Rocks that line the boundary are pulverized as the plates grind along, creating a linear fault valley or undersea canyon. Earthquakes are common along these faults.

20. how are hills and canyons formed?​


Most canyons were formed by a process of long-time erosion from a plateau or table-land level. The cliffs form because harder rock strata that are resistant to erosion and weathering remain exposed on the valley walls.

Hills are also formed because of erosion, which happens when bits of rock, soil, and sediment get washed away and placed in a pile somewhere else. Hills can be destroyed by erosion, as material is worn away by wind and water.

It is situated in the Yapak district, which is renowned for its tranquility, beautiful greenery and a stunning beach made out of tiny shells; and Balinghai Beach, a remote beach with a distinctive dining option. You won't have to travel far to fulfill your shopping needs because City mall is close by and the are restaurants and a movie theater in addition to a grocery and a retail shop. The Shangri-La resort boasts 2 private beaches that are a part of it, Punta Bunga and Banyuga Beach, in addition to being close to a number of lovely beaches. They cannot be accessed from outside the resort and are completely cut away from all public areas.

One of the top ten first-class resorts in the Philippines is Antulang Beach Resort. One of the resort's distinctive qualities is the winding staircase that leads to a deserted beach and visitors can take in the stunning sight of both the sunrise and the sunset from the twisting staircase. The hotel features its own underwater coral structures that support a marine eco-system. Billiards, horseback riding, diving and snorkeling, table tennis, kayaking, and rappelling are just a few of the recreational options the resort offers to its visitors.

The villa's name is called "Balai," the Filipino word for a thatched home. They are historic villas that are basic, yet elegant and rustic.
