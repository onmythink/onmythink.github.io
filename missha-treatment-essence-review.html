Law is a common ordinance of reasonability for the sake of common good amongst the community, formed by those who are in charge to care for such a community, and herald it.

Essence as defined in correlation to law states that reason must be used as a precondition to justice; to pursue something beneficial. We must also recognise the limits of our acts in which we direct our actions as an individual insuch a way that it meets both the ends of another and yourself.

Eternal Law - What God wills for creation, must return to him as a participant.

Natural Law - Change the direction of our actions based on the natural aptitude given by God.

Human Law - The rules artificially made by humans as a community to set boundaries between good and adverse actions.

Divine Law - A supernatural happiness that can be achieved solely through God; instances where we get instructions from divine revelation.
