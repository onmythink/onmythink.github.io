8. Find the speed of a plane traveled 6km in 4minutes in terms of m/s


Answer:

To find the speed of the plane, we need to divide the distance traveled by the time it took to travel that distance. In this case, the distance is 6 kilometers and the time is 4 minutes.

Since there are 60 seconds in one minute, the time in seconds would be 4 minutes * 60 seconds/minute = <<4*60=240>>240 seconds.

Now we can divide the distance by the time to find the speed of the plane: 6 kilometers / 240 seconds = 0.025 kilometers/second.

Since there are 1000 meters in one kilometer, the speed of the plane in meters per second would be 0.025 kilometers/second * 1000 meters/kilometer = 25 meters/second.

Therefore, the speed of the plane is 25 m/s.
