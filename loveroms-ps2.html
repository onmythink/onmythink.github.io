Answer:

The two photosystems absorb light energy through proteins containing pigments, such as chlorophyll. The light-dependent reactions begin in photosystem II. In PSII, energy from sunlight is used to split water, which releases two electrons, two hydrogen atoms, and one oxygen atom.

7. what interface is a standard for transmitting high-quality digital audio without going through an analog conversation process?a. PS2b. SPDIFc. USBd. VGA


Answer:

C. SPDIF

Explanation:

The interface that is a standard for transmitting high-quality digital audio without going through an analog conversation process is SPDIF (Sony/Philips Digital Interface Format). Therefore, the answer is b. SPDIF.

SPDIF is a common interface used to connect digital audio devices, such as CD/DVD players, computer sound cards, and home theater systems. It allows for the transfer of high-quality digital audio signals without any loss of quality that can occur during the analog-to-digital and digital-to-analog conversion processes.
