5. How many fifths are there in eleven


To determine the number of 1/5 there are in 11, divide 11 by 1/5.

11 ÷ [tex] \frac{1}{5} [/tex]

To divide the numbers, express 11 in a fraction.

= [tex] \frac{11}{1} [/tex] ÷ [tex] \frac{1}{5} [/tex]

And inorder to divide two fractions, flip the numerator and denominator in the second fraction and change the operation from division to multiplication.

= [tex] \frac{11}{1} [/tex] × [tex] \frac{5}{1} [/tex]

= [tex] \frac{55}{1} [/tex]

= 55

Therefore, there are 55 fifths in 11.

ANSWER: 115 kilometers

Explanation:

If Max travels 10 km per hour, we need to multiply 11 1/2 and 10 to know how far he could travel after 11 1/2 hours.

[tex]=11\frac{1}{2} \times10[/tex]

[tex]= \frac{23}{2} \times\frac{10}{1}[/tex]

[tex]= \frac{230}{2}[/tex]

[tex]= 115[/tex]

Therefore, Max can travel 115 kilometers after 11 1/2 hours.
