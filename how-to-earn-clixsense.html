4. A working student earns 25.50 per hour how much does he earn in 4hours if the works 3days a week how much does he earn in a week​


He earns 102.00 in 4 hours.He earns 1836.00 in a week.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

The problem is asking for the total amount that the working student earns in 4 hours and in a week. There are two steps to find the answer.

Find the amount he earns in 4 hours by multiplying the amount per hour by the number of hours. Find the total amount he earns in a week by multiplying the amount per hour by the number of hours in 3 days.

Given:

amount per hour - 25.50

number of hours - 4

days in a week - 3

Solution:

Step 1:

25.50 × 4 = 102.00

He earns 102.00 in 4 hours.

Step 2:

There are 24 hours in a day.

24 × 3 = 72 hours

72 × 25.50 = 1836.00

He earns 1836.00 in a week.

Final Answer:

102.001836.00

21. Veagara tore earnde 8700 a week how many i her average earning in a day.


Answer:

Problem Solving

Veagara Tore earned 8700 a week, how many is her average earning in a day?

To solve this problem, we need to consider the following:

One week have 7 days, if weekends is not considered as working days, then Veagara Tore should have to work in 5 days per week.To get her average earning in a day, we need to divide her weekly earnings by the number of days in a week.We will be having two solutions, the one is for seven days, and one is for five days. You should choose which one is suitable for your answer.

Solution:

(for 5 days)

8700 ÷ 5 = n8700 ÷ 5 = 1,740She earned 1,740 per day in one week (consisting of 5 days)

(for 7 days)

8700 ÷ 7 = n8700 ÷ 7 = 1,242.857 or 1,242.86 (rounded off into nearest hundredths or two decimal places)She earned 1,242.86 per day in one week (which consists of 7 days)
