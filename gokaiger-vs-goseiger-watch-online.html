Traditional libraries offer print and electronic versions of books, magazines, research databases and newspapers, but credible, trustworthy and reliable information also can be obtained electronically using online libraries. In most cases, information access generally depends on your preferences.

Objects can contain digitized content such as prints or photographs, as well as primarily produced digital content such as word processor files or social media posts.
