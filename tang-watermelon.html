13. A 4.6-kg watermelon is pushed across a table. If the acceleration of the watermelon is 3.2m/s² to the left, what is the net external force exerted on the watermelon?


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The force acting on an object is given by

[tex]\boxed{F = ma}[/tex]

where:

F = force

m = mass

a = acceleration

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for F

F = ma

F = (4.6 kg)(3.2 m/s²)

[tex]\boxed{F = \text{14.72 N}}[/tex]

Therefore, the net force exerted on the watermelon is 14.72 N.
