12. a portable fan uses 25 resistance connected to a power source. what would be the amount of voltage if it consumes a current of 4.0 A​


Voltage is 100 V

Voltage is 100 VExplanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

I = 4 A

R = 25 Ω

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Voltage

Step 3: Solve for Voltage

Solution:

The formula for Voltage is given by:

V = IR

where: V - Voltage

I - Current

R - Resistance

Substituting the value of I and R in the formula,

V = (4 A)(25 Ω)

V = 100 V
