11. What are the relative and vicinal location of Manila, Cebu and Davao?​


Answer:

Manila, Cebu, and Davao are cities in the Philippines.

Relative location refers to the location of a place in relation to other places. Manila is located north of Cebu and east of Davao. Cebu is located south of Manila and east of Davao. Davao is located west of both Manila and Cebu.

Vicinal location refers to the immediate surroundings of a place. Manila is located in a bay area and surrounded by several smaller islands. Cebu is located on the eastern coast of the island of Cebu and is surrounded by smaller islands as well. Davao is located in a gulf area and surrounded by several smaller islands too.

Absolute Location

Absolute location defines the position of a place based on a fixed point on Earth. The most traditional method is to distinguish the spot using coordinates such as latitude and longitude. Latitude indicates the north-south position of a point on Earth. On the other hand, longitude marks the position east-west of a point on Earth.
