I believe we can only come up with the veracious account of our history if the government will first obliterate the proliferation of fake news. It would be difficult to assert the truth of the past if we are competing with "fake news"  and the likes of historical revisionism in feeding our citizens with the truth of our history.  

The government on its part must see to it that information released and published by the media must be accurate, reliable, and should have no trace of fabrication. Thus these companies must only feed the public with news that are accurate and bias free.

It would have been great if the government would take the initiative to form a committee that is task to collect all historical facts about the Philippines and use these as standard and the ultimate source in Educating the people especially the youth about our history.
