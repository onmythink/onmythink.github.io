There's no doubt Marvel's Thor makes me review my knowledge about Norse Mythology.  And maybe that's the reason why I love the movie. It's not just about comics, it is also referring to a different culture, literature and beliefs of other race.  I love the movie's style which critic of The Atlantic said, "Perfectly acceptable as an action movie but inspired as a comedy". 

I like how Thor and Loki still got together (or end up as a team) even if they are each other's nemesis: The God of Thunder and the God of Mischief.  But I can't also deny the fact that Cate Blanchett (as Hela) and Tessa Thompson (as Valkyrie) stole mega-attention from viewers because of their girl-power.  They did very well.
