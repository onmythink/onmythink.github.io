Carlo = Karla × 2

Karla = ?

Kent = (Carlo + Karla) × 2

So,

Carlo is 2

Karla is 1

Kent is 6

Total: 9

So, 9 divided to 45,000

is 5,000

So if,

Karla saved up 5,000

And Carlo saved up as twice as Karla 5,000 × 2 = 10,000
