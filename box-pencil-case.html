Answer:

C. ( m²+6m-40) pencils

Step-by-step explanation:

(m+10) (m-4)

[tex]m \times m = {m }^{2} \\ m \times -4 = - 4m \\ m \times 10 = 10m \\ 10 \times - 4 = - 40 \\ \\ 10m + ( - 4m) = 6 \\ [/tex]

thus the answer is m²+6m-40
