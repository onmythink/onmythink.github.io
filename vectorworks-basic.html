Mode of transmission

Ang mode of transmission ay ang paraan ng interpretasyon o paglipat ng transporter (causative espesyalista) sa ibang tao sa pamamagitan ng mga patak, airborne, foodborne, vectorborne, at bloodborne. Ang mode of transmission ay maaringmaipasa ng (direkta) o sa paikot-ikot na paraan (backhanded) na pakikipag-ugnayan tulad ng mga lamok, karayom, at paggamit ng mga indibidwal na gamit. Sa pamamagitan ng mode of transmission naililipat o naisasalin natin ang iba't ibang uri ng mikrobyo sa ibang tao na maaring magdulot ng pagkakasakit. Ang mode of transmission ay nangyayari lamang kung ang iyong sakit ay nakahahawa. Ang tamang pag-alaga sa katawan at sarili ay nakatutulong upang hindi tayo lipatan ng mga mikrobyo.

29. Basic strokes and basic rules of tennis​


Answer:

Basic Strokes:

• Forehand: Hit the ball with the face of the racket, using a swinging motion.

• Backhand: Hit the ball with the back of the racket, using a swinging motion.

• Serve: Hit the ball over the net into the other court.

• Volley: Hit the ball before it bounces on the court.

Basic Rules of Tennis:

• The ball must be hit over the net and into the opposing court.

• The ball must bounce in the court once before it is hit by either player.

• The ball can only be hit with the racket and not with any other body part.

• A point is scored when the ball lands outside of the court or when the opponent misses the ball.

• A set is won when a player wins 6 games with a minimum difference of two games.

• A match is won when a player wins two out of three sets.
