Distance: 648 miles

Time: 24 hrs

Speed: n

Remember the formulas in finding speed, distance, and time:

S = D ÷ T

D = S × T

T = D ÷ S

Given:

Distance: 648 miles

Time: 24 hrs

Speed: n

Solution:

S = D ÷ T

S = 648 ÷ 24

S = 27 mi/h

Thus, the speed is 27 mi/h
