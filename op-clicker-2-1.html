24. what is the proper way to hold a bamboo pole to avoid hurting the fingers of the clickers


The clickers sit on the floor on their knees or with legs crossed. In front of their feet is the beam and on top are the ends of the poles.  The clickers sit about 8 feet apart.  They hold the poles in their hands and perform the three count rhythm. On count 1, the poles are tapped on the beam, on count 2 the poles are tapped on the beam and on count 3 the poles are slide together. The cue is - tap tap close, tap tap close, or open open close, open open close.  The clickers maintain a steady tempo throughout the dance.
