8. If the pressure inside a rubber balloon is 1500 mmHg, what is this pressure in pounds-force per square inch (psi)?


[tex]\tt{\huge{\blue{Explanation:}}}[/tex]

The conversion factors we need to convert from mmHg to psi are

1 atm = 760 mmHg

1 atm = 14.696 psi

[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Solving for the pressure in psi

[tex]P = \text{1,500 mmHg} \times \dfrac{\text{1 atm}}{\text{760 mmHg}} \times \dfrac{\text{14.696 psi}}{\text{1 atm}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{P = \text{29 psi}}[/tex]

Therefore, the pressure in psi is 29 psi.
