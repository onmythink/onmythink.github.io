24. what is meant by mystical body of christ?and why it i called mystical?​


Answer:

A third aspect of our union with Christ is what some call the mystical or personal union. This is the personal indwelling of the believer by faith through the person and work of the Holy Spirit. A number of passages speak to this dimension of our union with Christ, including Ephesians 2, where the Apostle Paul explains that we are members of the household of God, built on the foundation of the Apostles and prophets, with Christ as the cornerstone. About this grand and final temple, Paul writes that we grow “into a holy temple in the Lord,” and “in him [we] also are being built together into a dwelling place for God by the Spirit”
