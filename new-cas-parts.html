1. 5. what are the congruent corresponding parts of side CA?​


Answer:

If two triangles are congruent, then the sides and angles that match are called corresponding parts. Here, angle A corresponds to angle D, angle B corresponds to angle F, and angle C corresponds to angle E. Side AB corresponds to side DF, side BC corresponds to side FE, and side CA corresponds to side ED.

Determining Ratio

Ratios determine how a quantity is related to another quantity. It tells how many times larger the quantity than the other quantity. It can be expressed in three different ways: word form, colon form and fraction form.

Word Form (a to b)

Colon Form (a:b)

Fraction Form (a/b)

Ratios are also expressed in their simplest form. Just divide the quantities by their greatest common factor.

Let us now answer the given activity above.

1) The ratio of 8 triangles is to 6 trapezoids:

Its ratio is 8:6, however, you can still simplify it since they have a common factor which is 2. Divide both sides by 2.

8÷2 : 6÷2 = 4:3

The ratio is 4:3, 4 to 3 or ‌4⁄3‌.

2) The ratio of 10 arrow ups is to 8 arrow downs:

Its ratio is 10:8, however, you can still simplify it since they have a common factor which is 2. Divide both sides by 2.

10÷2 : 8÷2 = 5:4

The ratio is 5:4, 5 to 4 or ‌5⁄4.

3) The ratio of 4 triangles is to 5 squares:

The ratio is 4:5, 4 to 5 or ‌4⁄5.

4) The ratio of 10 decimeters to 10 centimeters:

Notice that the two quantities have different units. To find the ratio, you have to make their unit similar first by converting decimeters to centimeters.

1 dm = 10 cm

10 dm = 10 × 10 cm = 100 cm

The ratio is 100:10. However, you can still simplify them by dividing both sides by 10.

100÷10 : 10÷10 = 10:1

The ratio is 10:1, 10 to 1 or ‌10⁄1.

5) The ratio of 6 weeks to 12 days:

Notice that the two quantities have different units. To find the ratio, you have to make their unit similar first by converting weeks to days.

1 week = 7 days

6 weeks = 6 × 7 = 42

The ratio is 42:12. However, you can still simplify them by dividing both sides by 6.

42÷6 : 12÷6 = 7:2

The ratio is 7:2, 7 to 2 or ‌‌7⁄2.

The ionic bond between Calcium (Ca) from Group IIA and Sulfur (S) from Group VIA is formed by the transfer of electrons.
