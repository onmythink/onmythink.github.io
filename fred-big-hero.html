21. gave one heroes a big contribution attained Philippines independece​


Answer:

Andres Bonifacio

Explanation:

Andres Bonifacio or Andrés Bonifacio y de Castro was a Filipino revolutionary leader and the president of the Tagalog Republic. He is often called "The Father of the Philippine Revolution". He was one of the founders and later Supremo (Supreme Leader) of the Kataas-taasang, Kagalang-galangang Katipunan ng mga Anak ng Bayan or more commonly known as the "Katipunan", a movement which sought the independence of the Philippines from Spanish colonial rule and started the Philippine Revolution. He was also one of the Filipino historical figures to be recommended as a national hero of the Philippines.

29. 10. Consider the following statements:All piano players are musicians.Fred is a piano player.Which of the following is a conclusion which can be drawn from the statements?A. All piano players are named Fred.B. All musicians are piano players.C. Fred is a musician.D. Fred is not a musician.​


ARGUMENTS, PREMISES, AND CONCLUSIONS

The conclusion which can be drawn from the statements is Fred is a musician (C).

Logic is the discipline that assesses arguments.An argument is a collection of claims with one or more premises and one, and only one, conclusion.A statement is a phrase that can be either true or incorrect, such as "The cat is on the mat." Many phrases, such as "Close the door, please," and "How old are you?" are not sentences.A claim in an argument that justifies or supports the conclusion is referred to as the premise. An argument may contain one or more premises.A conclusion is a sentence in an argument that expresses what the speaker is seeking to persuade the reader or listener to believe. What does the argument seek to demonstrate? In a single argument, there can only be one possible conclusion.
