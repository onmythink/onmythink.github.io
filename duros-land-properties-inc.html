Answer:

19 hectares

Step-by-step explanation:

convert the ff. mixed fractions into improper fraction

598 1/2 will become 1197/2 while 15 3/4 will become 63/4

then divide the 2 improper fraction

1197/2 ÷ 63/4 = 19 hectares
