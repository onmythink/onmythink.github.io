18. fred is walking at 1.7 m/s, he sees a dollar and runs at 2.5 m/s in 1.5 seconds. what is fred's acceleration​


Explanation:

First, subtract the other two velocities to get the final velocity:

2.5 m/sec - 1.7 m/sec = 0.8 m/sec

Using that velocity, us the formula a = v/t

0.8 m/sec ÷ 1.5 sec = 0.5 m/sec^2

0.5 m/sec^2 is the answer.

30. 10. Consider the following statements:All piano players are musicians.Fred is a piano player.Which of the following is a conclusion which can be drawn from the statements?A. All piano players are named Fred.B. All musicians are piano players.C. Fred is a musician.D. Fred is not a musician.​


ARGUMENTS, PREMISES, AND CONCLUSIONS

The conclusion which can be drawn from the statements is Fred is a musician (C).

Logic is the discipline that assesses arguments.An argument is a collection of claims with one or more premises and one, and only one, conclusion.A statement is a phrase that can be either true or incorrect, such as "The cat is on the mat." Many phrases, such as "Close the door, please," and "How old are you?" are not sentences.A claim in an argument that justifies or supports the conclusion is referred to as the premise. An argument may contain one or more premises.A conclusion is a sentence in an argument that expresses what the speaker is seeking to persuade the reader or listener to believe. What does the argument seek to demonstrate? In a single argument, there can only be one possible conclusion.

GIVEN

A. All piano players are named Fred.

B. All musicians are piano players.

C. Fred is a musician.

Solution

Since Fred is a piano player and all piano players are musicians, that means Fred is also a musician.

Therefore, Fred is a musician.
