<------Larger-----Smaller------>
K-----H-----Dc-----Basic-----D-----C-----M
This is the number line.

Move the decimal point of the given number on how you move it from the unit to the new unit.

28. What monasteries, convents, and castles converted into hotels are called?


Monasteries, convents, and castles converted into hotels are called paradors.

Explanation :

Paradors is a hotel built on the site of a former monastery or castle. Several famous paradors are located in the Mediterranean countries such as Greece, Italy, Croatia, Morocco and the most famous for having various paradors is Spain.

Paradors are generally categorized as luxury hotels, paradors are usually located in converted historical buildings such as monasteries or castles. However, some Paradors in Spain have a more modern concept or building background. Most paradors are built in locations with panoramic views and special locations.

Because it is classified as a luxury feature, the price of Paradors can be considered quite expensive. In 2021 prices are around 600 euros for 1 or 2 people. This equates to 120 euros per night including breakfast.
