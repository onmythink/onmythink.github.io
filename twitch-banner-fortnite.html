12. describe muscle twitch, summation, tetanus and recruitment?​


muscle twitch

Muscle twitching is also called muscle fasciculation. Twitching involves small muscle contractions in the body. Your muscles are made up of fibers that your nerves control. Stimulation or damage to a nerve may cause your muscle fibers to twitch. Most muscle twitches go unnoticed and aren't cause for concern.

summation

The production of force through the sequential movement of various parts of the body in order to produce the optimum amount.

Tetanus

Tetanus, also called lockjaw, is a serious infection caused by Clostridium tetani. This bacterium produces a toxin that affects the brain and nervous system, leading to stiffness in the muscles. If Clostridium tetani spores are deposited in a wound, the neurotoxin interferes with nerves that control muscle movement.

Recruitment

Recruitment refers to the overall process of identifying, attracting, screening, shortlisting, and interviewing, suitable candidates for jobs within an organization. Recruitment can also refer to processes involved in choosing individuals for unpaid roles.

The shape of a banner is a rectangle so we need to apply the basics of rectangles in order to answer this.

[tex]\tt \overline{ GE} = \overline{MO}[/tex]

The length of the banner is either the measure of [tex]\tt \overline {GE}[/tex] or [tex]\tt \overline {MO}[/tex]. You can choose one. I'll be using [tex]\tt \overline {GE}[/tex].

[tex]\tt \overline {MO} = 9x+3\\\tt \overline {MO} = 9(13)+3\\\tt \overline {MO} =117+3\\\tt \overline {MO} =120 \ in[/tex]

Therefore, the length of the banner is [tex]\large \boldsymbol {\tt 120 \ in}[/tex].

c. Find the width of the banner.

The width of the banner is the measure of [tex]\tt \overline {GM}[/tex].

[tex]\tt \overline {GM} = 5x+15[/tex]

[tex]\tt \overline {GM} = 80 \ in[/tex]

Therefore, the width of the banner is [tex]\large \boldsymbol {\tt 80 \ in}[/tex].

The perimeter of the rectangle is the sum of the measure of the sides.

30. Twitch is a spinoff of what website?


Answer:

Justin.tv

Explanation:

Twitch is a live streaming service that was a spin-off, or based from a more general-interest streaming platform called Justin.tv. This stream service is operated by Twitch Interactive which is a subsidiary of Amazon.

Justin.tv primarily was focused on video game live streaming. This also included broadcasts of e-Sports tournaments as well as music, creative content and reality-based broadcasts. The contents are available either live as as video on demand. Video on demand is like Netflix where one could simply select from a selection of options and then watch, pause and resume at any given time.

Twitch became more popular than Justin.tv and data says that by 2015 it had over 1.5 million broadcasters and hundreds of millions of viewers on a monthly basis. Twitch likewise has partnered with over 27,000 channels giving viewers a wide array of options available when going into their platform.

Note that Twitch is a subsidiary of Amazon, which is considered as one of the Big Four companies in the United States. Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos.
