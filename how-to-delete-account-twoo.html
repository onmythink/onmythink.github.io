[tex] \tt \frac{x \times 6}{100} = \frac{6x}{100} \\ [/tex]

And interest for 7% rate

[tex] \tt \frac{(18000 - x) \times 7}{100} \\ [/tex]

So the total interest is

[tex] \tt \frac{6x}{100} + \frac{(18000 - x) \times 7}{100} = 1190[/tex]

[tex] \implies \tt6x + 126000 - 7x = 119000[/tex]

[tex]\implies \tt x = 126000 - 119000[/tex]

[tex]\implies \tt x = 7000[/tex]

So she invested php 7000 in 6% rate and php (18000-7000) = php 11000 in 7% rate
