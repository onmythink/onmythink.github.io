25. franchezka Razielle has 1/4 bibingka. Her Mother shared 1/8 with her classmate. What part of the bibingka was left?


Answer: 1/8

Step-by-step explanation:

Franchezka Razielle has 1/4 bibingka. Her Mother shared 1/8 with her classmate. What part of the bibingka was left?

Our first step is to convert this word problem into a mathematical problem.

"How to convert?"

Understand the problem. It says that Razielle has 1/4 bibingka and her mother shared 1/8 of the bibingka to her classmate. That means she TOOK 1/8 bibingka from the 1/4 bibingka.

So that means we subtract 1/4 by 1/8

*subtracting fractions*

We cannot subtract fractions if they have different denominators. So we have to make them similar. To do that, we multiply the denominator 8 to the other denominator 4.

The numerators also have to change so we simply multiply them to their denominators.

So we multiply 1 by 8, and get 8.

Then we multiply 1 by 4, and get 4.

Next we give both terms new denominators -- 4 × 8 = 32.

So now our fractions look like this:

8/32 - 4/32

Now that our denominators match, we can now subtract the numerators.

4/32

We subtracted the numerators but COPIED the denominatir because they are similar

"can we reduce the number to simpler fraction?"

To find out, we try dividing the numerator AND denominator by 2

4/32÷2 = 2/16

Let's try dividing it by 2 again...

2/16÷2 = 1/8

Let's try dividing it by 2 again...

Nope, we can't. Because 2 is greater than numerator 1.

Therefore, our final answer is 1/8
