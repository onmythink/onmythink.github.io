The rancher has a small boat that will only stay afloat carrying the rancher and one of the critters or the rancher and the carrots. The rancher cannot leave the dog alone with the rabbit because the dog will eat the rabbit. The rancher cannot leave the rabbit alone with the carrots because the rabbit will eat the carrots. How can the rancher get across the river with the critters and the carrots?


Answer:

Legend:

R:Rancher

Rb:Rabbit

D:Dog

C:Carrot

First Trip:

R and Rb will cross the river.R will leave Rb on the other side.The other side will be fine,since D won't eat C.

Second Trip:

R will now carry C across the river.However,upon reaching the other side,leave C and carry Rb on the boat again.Get back to the bank where D is.

Third Trip:

Carry D on the boat and leave Rb on the bank.Cross the river and leave D with C.Get back to the other side again.

Fourth Trip:

Carry Rb and cross the river again.
