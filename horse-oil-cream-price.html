30. An ice cream machine is being sold by a couple since they wanted to venture into another business. They were trying to sell it for 10% off of the marked price but no one wanted to buy it. Then they tried to sell for 20% of the first sale price still no one bought the machine. Finally, they offered it for 25% off the second sale price when someone bought it for P32,400.00. What was the original price of the ice cream machine?​


Answer:

The original price of the ice cream machine was, 60,000 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's denote the original price of the ice cream machine as x.

According to the problem, the couple tried to sell it for 10% off the marked price, which means they were trying to sell it for 0.9x. However, no one wanted to buy it at that price.

Then they tried to sell it for 20% off the first sale price, which is equivalent to selling it for 0.8(0.9x) = 0.72x. Again, no one bought it.

Finally, they offered it for 25% off the second sale price, which means the machine was sold for 0.75(0.72x) = 0.54x = P32,400.00.

To find x, we can solve for it:

0.54x = P32,400.00

x = P32,400.00 / 0.54

x = P60,000.00

Therefore, the original price of the ice cream machine was P60,000.00.
