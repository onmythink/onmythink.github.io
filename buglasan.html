Explanation:

1. The Sinulog Festival is a festival originating in Cebu City in the Philippines, celebrating the Cebuanos' conversion to Roman Catholicism; it is held on the third Sunday of January and honors the Santo Niňo— the Santo Niňo de Cebú—or Holy Child Jesus.

2.Buglasan is the province's annual Provincial Fiesta that is held every October. It is a provincial event where in all the municipalities, cities and towns of the Negros Oriental province becomes one. It is actually a unique celebration because it is a gathering of almost all the people in the province.

3.The Sandugo Festival is an annual historical celebration that takes place every year in Tagbilaran City on the island of Bohol in the Philippines. This festival commemorates the Treaty of Friendship between Datu Sikatuna, a chieftain in Bohol, and Spanish conquistador Miguel López de Legazpi

4.Held during September, the Solili Festival is a marriage ritual where both parents of the married partners are presented with a feast and lectures about the realities and hardships of their married life. The Solili Festival rituals are depicted through street dancing competition.

15. 1. Unsa ang sinugdanan sa Sandugo Festival sa Bohol?A. Blood CompactB. Battle of MactanC. BuglasanD. Battle of War​


Ang simula sa Sandugo Festival sa Bohol ay Blood Compact.

Tungkol sa sandugo festival

Ang Sandugo Festival ay isang taunang makasaysayang pagdiriwang na nagaganap taun-taon sa Tagbilaran City sa isla ng Bohol sa Pilipinas. Ang pagdiriwang na ito ay ginugunita ang Treaty of Friendship sa pagitan ni Datu Sikatuna, isang chieftain sa Bohol, at Spanish conquistador Miguel López de Legazpi. Ang kasunduang pangkapayapaan noong ika-16 na siglo ay naganap noong Marso 16, 1565 sa pamamagitan ng isang blood compact o "sandugo".

Ang Sandugo Festival ay ginaganap tuwing Hulyo. Ang Tagbilaran City Charter Day sa Hulyo 1 ay sinisimulan ang isang buwang pagdiriwang na may isang banal na misa, diana, motorcade at programa na itinataguyod ng Pamahalaang Lungsod ng Tagbilaran. Kabilang sa mga pangunahing aktibidad sa buwan ay ang Miss Bohol Sandugo Beauty Pageant, at ang Sandugo Street Dancing Competition na karaniwang ginaganap tuwing ika-3 o ika-4 na Linggo ng Hulyo, at inorganisa ng Bohol Sandugo Foundation, Inc. (BSFI).

Province of Negros Oriental

Negros Oriental is a relaxed province in the southeastern part of Negros Island, Visayas, Philippines. The province is surrounded on the north and west by its neighboring province, Negros Occidentales, and is a breathtaking blend of culture and modernization. Negros Oriental's capital is Dumaguete City. The government is also located in the city's capitol area. The Taon Strait separates the province from Cebu Island. To the east were Siquijor Island and Apo Island, both dive destinations. The northern parts of Mindanao can be seen from the south of Negros Oriental in good conditions.

The Buglasan Festival

1.)The Buglasan Festival celebrates the history, progress, and diversity of the Negros Oriental people's cultural richness. This is usually celebrated as an annual week-long festival every second week of October.

2) The Buglasan of Negros Oriental is one method of promoting and preserving other types of heritage, such as dances, songs, foods, rituals, and stories.

3.)The event, dubbed the BUGLASAN FESTIVAL in honor of the island's beginnings, when its cultural character and heritage began to take shape, included booth presentations, indigenous and growing culinary competitions, street dances, and field presentations of various local cultural productions that had evolved into modest festivals.

Province of Negros Oriental

Negros Oriental is a relaxed province in the southeastern part of Negros Island, Visayas, Philippines. The province is surrounded on the north and west by its neighboring province, Negros Occidentales, and is a breathtaking blend of culture and modernization. Negros Oriental's capital is Dumaguete City. The government is also located in the city's capitol area. The Taon Strait separates the province from Cebu Island. To the east were Siquijor Island and Apo Island, both dive destinations. The northern parts of Mindanao can be seen from the south of Negros Oriental in good conditions.

The Buglasan FestivalThe Buglasan Festival celebrates the history, progress, and diversity of the Negros Oriental people's cultural richness. This is usually celebrated as an annual week-long festival every second week of October.The Buglasan of Negros Oriental is one method of promoting and preserving other types of heritage, such as dances, songs, foods, rituals, and stories. The event, dubbed the BUGLASAN FESTIVAL in honor of the island's beginnings, when its cultural character and heritage began to take shape, included booth presentations, indigenous and growing culinary competitions, street dances, and field presentations of various local cultural productions that had evolved into modest festivals.
