20. What is the function of Shutter release button​


Question:

What is the function of Shutter release button

Answer:

Turns the camera on or off

Shutter-release button (sometimes just shutter release or shutter button) is a push-button found on many cameras, used to record photographs.When pressed, the shutter of the camera is "released", so that it opens to capture a picture, and then closes, allowing an exposure time as determined by the shutter speed setting (which may be automatic). Some cameras also utilize an electronic shutter, as opposed to a mechanical shutter.

The shutter-release button is one of the most basic features of a dedicated handheld camera.

The term "release" comes from old mechanical shutters that were "cocked" or "tensioned" by one lever, and then "released" by another.
