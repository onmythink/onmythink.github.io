Answer:

REPUBLIC ACT 7581 or the Price Act was signed into law on May 27, 1992. It took effect on June 7 of that year. The law provides consumer protection by stabilizing the price and supply of basic necessities and prime commodities and prescribing measures against undue price increases especially during emergencies.
