14. Water faucet how it work Cork opener how it work


Answer:

Water faucet how it work

Handles that need a twisting motion are connected to the plugging mechanism with a spiral device that allows it to slowly twist into the hole. Moving only a fraction of an inch for every revolution of the handle, twist-based facets make it easy to control the flow of water.

Cork opener how it work

Basic. In its traditional form, a corkscrew is simply a steel screw attached to a perpendicular handle, made of wood or some other material. The user grips the handle and screws the metal point into the cork, until the helix is firmly embedded, then a vertical pull on the corkscrew extracts the cork from the bottle.
