1. Photos were taken by me at the Baluarte ni Chavit Singson.Active or Passive​


Answer:

Passive

Explanation:

In a passive sentence, the person or thing doing the action (the actor) is usually preceded by the word "by."

Photos were taken BY me.

-'photos' is the subject
