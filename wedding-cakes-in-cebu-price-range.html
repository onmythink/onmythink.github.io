17. Vinsmoke Sanji purchased a red carpet with an area of 16,443 square meters in preparation for his wedding to Charlotte Pudding. The wedding cake of Whole Cake Island is rectangular is shape with the length of 189 meters. (1) Find the perimeter of the carpet. (2) If he wants to have a soldier stationed in every 12 meters, how many soldiers does he need? Show your illustrations.


To find the perimeter of the carpet, we need to find the length and width of the carpet and then add them together twice. We don't have enough information to find the length and width of the carpet, so we can't find the perimeter.

To find the number of soldiers Sanji needs, we need to first find the total length of the carpet. We know that the area of the carpet is 16,443 square meters and the length of the cake is 189 meters, so we can set up the following equation to find the width of the carpet:

16,443 = 189 * w

Solving for w, we find that the width of the carpet is 87 meters. Therefore, the total length of the carpet is 2 * (189 + 87) = 446 meters.

To find the number of soldiers Sanji needs, we can divide the total length of the carpet by the length of the area that each soldier is responsible for:

446 / 12 = 37 soldiers

So Sanji will need 37 soldiers to station along the carpet.

Problem:

Today is Joseph's wedding. He went to the bakeshop to pick-up his wedding cake. He instructed the baker that the cross-section area of each of the two bottom layers of the wedding cake is double to cross section area of the layer on top and the three layers have the same height.

Asked:

What is the volume of the cake?

Solution:

Information to keep in mindr₁ = 20cm (bottom layer)r₂ = 20/10 = 10cm (middle layer)r₃ = 10/2 = 5cm (top layer)h (of each layer) = 15cm

Formula of a cylinderV = πr²h

Volume (total)V = πr₁²h + πr₂²h + πr₃²hV = πh (r₁² + r₂² + r₃²)V = 3.14 x 15 ([20]² + [10]² + [5]²)V = 47.1 (400 + 100 + 25)V = 47.1 (525)V = 24,727.5 cm³

Answer:

Therefore, The volume of the cake is 24,727.5 cm³.
