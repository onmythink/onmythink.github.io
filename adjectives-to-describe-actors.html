11. Which of the following theatrical elements of sarswela describes the wardrobe used by the actors for their performance


Answer:

The elements of the Philippine Zarzuela are that there are male and female main actors and assisting actors dressed in different outfits depending on the theme of the story and on the roles of the actors. It is performed on either a theater or stage with elaborate props and grand stage designs that emphasize the story of the Zarzuela. The area is very spacious where actors and actresses can move freely as they portray their respective roles and depict the scene. The props, stage designs, and costumes are colorful. It is also accompanied by music played by an orchestra. The music in the zarzuela helps portray the meaning of the text sang by the actors and actresses and the mood of the scene.
