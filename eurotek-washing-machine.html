21. The cost of 6 refrigirators and 5 washing machines is $23639.the cost of each refrigirator is $1650 more than the cost of a washing machine. How much does a washing machine cost?


Washing machine: x     Number of units: 5

Refrigerator: x + 1,650  Number of units: 6

(The refrigerator is 1,650 costs more than a washing machine.)

Total Cost: 23,639.00

Find x, the unit cost of washing machine:

5x + 6(x+1,650) = 23,639    ⇒   Equation

5x + 6x + 9,900 = 23,639

11x = 23,639 - 9,900

11x/11  = 13,739/11

x = 1,249   ⇒  the unit cost of washing machine

ANSWER: The unit cost of washing machine is $1,249.

Cost of a refrigerator: $1,249 + $1,650 = $2,899

Check:

5(washing machine) + 6(refrigerators) = 23,639

5(1,249) + 6(2,899) = 23,639

6,245 + 17,394 = 23,639

23,639 = 23,639   (True)

Let r = cost of 1 refrigerator, and w = cost of 1 washing machine.

"The cost of 6 refrigerator and 5 washing machines is $23639" can be written as: 6r + 5w = 23639, call this equation 1.

"the cost of each refrigerator is $1650 more than the cost of a washing machine" can be written as: r = 1650 + w, call this equation 2.

Replace r in equation 1 with equation 2 so: 6(1650 + w) + 5w = 23639

9900 + 11w = 23639

w = (23639 - 9900) / 11

w = 1249

and with the result of w we use equation 2 to work out r, r = 1650 + 1249

r = 2899

24. hand washing and machine washing Do's and don't ​


Answer:

Do's:

•Wet your hands thoroughly under clean running water, either warm or cold.

•Apply mild soap to hands and work into a lather, rubbing for at least 20 seconds.

•Cover the surface of your hands, including the front and back, the wrists, between fingers and under fingernails.Rinse your hands well.

•Dry hands thoroughly with an air dryer or clean paper towel.

Don't:

•Use hot water to wash hands. It tends to promote chapping which can make hands more susceptible to bacteria.

•Run water over hands when lathering.

•Touch the sink surface after washing hands.

•Skip handwashing if you don’t have soap and water. An alcohol-based hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol can be a replacement.
