4. this accurs when blood vessels in the brain are blocked or broken.​


Answer:

Stroke

A stroke occurs when a blood vessel in the brain becomes blocked or bursts. The brain relies on a network of blood vessels to provide it with blood that is rich in oxygen. In a stroke, there is a lack of blood supply that causes surrounding nerve cells to be cut off from their supply of nutrients and oxygen.

11. How do you translate broken chords from a guitar to piano notes ?


As a pianist, this guitar broken chord in piano is like a chord broken into sequence of notes. One type of broken chord in the what we call 'arpeggio.' For example, a C major chord is composed of Do(C) Mi(E) Sol(G). Sometimes, we add the 'TONIC' or the first note, thus resulting to Do-Mi-Sol-Do. Back to the topic, if we turn the C major chord CEG into arpeggio, then you play on the piano key the notes Do(C), Mi(E) and Sol(G) (C E G E C). Sometimes, we play the full chord, including the tonic like C E G C G E C.
