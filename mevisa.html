Answer:

The face-to-face relation (French: rapport de face à face) is a concept in the French philosopher Emmanuel Lévinas' thought on human sociality. It means that, ethically, people are responsible to one-another in the face-to-face encounter. Specifically, Lévinas says that the human face "orders and ordains" us.

12. Melisa poured 9L of juice equally into 24 bottles.What is the volume, in L, of juice in each bottle?


The solution is:
9 / 24
(9 / 3) / (24 / 3)
3 / 8

3/8 in decimal form is 0.375

So therefore, there are 0.375L of juice in each bottle, or 3/8 to be more exact.

18. This week Melisa studied a total of 28 hours for her PSAT. This total was more than four times the amount of hours she studied last week. How many hours did Melisa studied last week?​


Answer:

7 hours Step-by-step explanation:

At first let's understanding the question situation,

This week Melisa studied a total of 28 hours for her PSAT.

So,This week studies=28 hours

According to the question,

This total was more than four times the amount of hours she studied last week.

And we have to find How many hours did Melisa studied last week.

Solution:-

[tex]\sf{This~week ~study=4\times last ~week~study}\\\\\sf{28=4\times last~week~study}\\\\\sf{last~week~study=\dfrac{28}{4}}\\\\\sf{last~week~study=\bold{7~hours}}[/tex]

Final answer:-

∴ Melisa studied last week is 7 Hours.
