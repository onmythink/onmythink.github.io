Recycling one ton of office paper can save the energy equivalent of consuming 322 gallons of gasoline.

How does recycling save energy?

When we make new products out of virgin materials, we expend energy to extract and process those materials. This includes burning fossil fuels. 28. Joel biked for 3/4 noel biked 1/2 of hour more than Joel. Aannel bling . How many hour did Noel bike​


ADDITION OF DISSIMILAR FRACTIONS

Problem:

Joel biked for 3/4 hour. Noel biked 1/2 hour more than Joel. How many hours did Noel bike​?

Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

This word problem involves addition of dissimilar fractions. Dissimilar fractions are referred to as fractions with different denominators. We can only add them if we convert them to similar fractions. In this case, we need to add 3/4 and 1/2 to obtain the total number of hours that Noel biked.

So:

3/4 + 1/2

By finding for the LCD or the least common multiple of their denominators, we can turn them into similar fractions. The LCM of 2 and 4 is also 4. So with this, we will transform 1/2 to its equivalent fraction with 4 at its denominator. Just simply divide the LCD which is 4 by 2 then multiply to the numerator of 1/2 which is 1. So, 1/2 is equal to 2/4.

Since both fractions have already the same denominator, we can now directly add the numerators then copy the denominator.

[tex]\frac{3}{4} +\frac{2}{4} = \frac{3+2}{4} = \frac{5}{4} = 1\frac{1}{4}[/tex]

As we add them, we got 5/4 but we need to make it a mixed number so it is 1 1/4.

Therefore, Noel biked for 1 and 1/4 hours.
