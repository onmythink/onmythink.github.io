9. Explain briefly ypur ideas about the following questions 1.What is logo? 2.What are the three types of logo? 3.What is personal logo?4.what is crest logo? 5.what is business or product logo? ​


Answer:

1. A logo is a graphic mark, emblem, or symbol used to aid and promote public identification and recognition. It may be of an abstract or figurative design or include the text of the name it represents as in a wordmark

2. Now that we've covered the three main types of logos (wordmark, monogram, and combination mark), we'll talk about two less common types of logos.

3. Personal logos are the artist's own brand. An artist's personal logo are an extension of the artist's ethos or character. It acts as the graphic representation of the artist or the artist's work.

4. Crest logos are rooted in deep symbolism. Often designed after the elaborate crests made in medieval Europe, their basic makeup usually involves a shield, scroll, or two mirrored animals. Designs may also vary, with elaborate curves or any swash details to accompany the main insignia

5. A corporate logo represents your business as a whole. It reflects your company's name, vision, and mission all in one iconic graphic. In contrast, a product logo is a graphic made for one specific product or line of products made by your company.
