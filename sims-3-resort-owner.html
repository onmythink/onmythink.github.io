23. last summer the entrance fee in sandy's resort is 50 pesos for adults and 30 pesos for children .If 100 when to the resort one day during summer and the owner collected 3180 pesos .How many of them were adults and how many were children​


Step-by-step explanation:

Let's assume that the number of adults who went to the resort was "A" and the number of children was "C".

From the problem, we know that the entrance fee for adults is 50 pesos and for children is 30 pesos. We also know that 100 people went to the resort and the owner collected a total of 3180 pesos.

Using this information, we can set up two equations:

A + C = 100 (equation 1, because the total number of people who went to the resort was 100)

50A + 30C = 3180 (equation 2, because the owner collected a total of 3180 pesos)

We can use equation 1 to solve for A in terms of C:

A = 100 - C

Substituting this expression for A into equation 2, we get:

50(100 - C) + 30C = 3180

Expanding the brackets and simplifying, we get:

5000 - 20C = 3180

Subtracting 5000 from both sides, we get:

-20C = -1820

Dividing both sides by -20, we get:

C = 91

Therefore, 91 children went to the resort that day. We can use equation 1 to find the number of adults:

A + C = 100

A + 91 = 100

A = 9

Therefore, 9 adults went to the resort that day.

In summary, there were 9 adults and 91 children who went to the resort, and the owner collected a total of 3180 pesos.

An owner has a rectangular lot with an area of 70 square meters. If the lot is x meters long and (x-3) meters wide, how long and how wide is the rectangular lot?​


The rectangular lot is 10 meters long and 7 meters wide.

Solution:

70=x(x-3)

70=x²-3x

x²-3x-70=0

x=10

Long= x= 10

Wide= x-3= 10-3=7

Answer:

The rectangular lot is 10m long and 7m wide.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Area: 70 m²

Length: x

Width: x-3

Area formula: a=lw

Solution:

70=(x)(x-3)

70=x²-3x ➡️ Write it as a standard form

-x²+3x+70=0 ➡️ change the signs

x²-3x-70=0 ➡️ write - 3x as a difference

x²+7x-10x-70=0 ➡️ factor out

x(x+7) - 10(x+7) = 0 ➡️ factor out x and -10 from the equation

(x+7)(x-10)=0 ➡️ find the roots

x+7=0 ; x-10=0

x=-7 ; x=10

Since there is no negative measurement, we have 10 and 7 as our measurements. The value of length (x) is 10m while the value of width (x-3) is 7m since we subracted 3 from 10 which is the x.
