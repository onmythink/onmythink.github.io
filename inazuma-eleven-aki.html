19. Aki is g centimeter tall. Pierre’s height is 4 less than thrice the height of Aki. The difference between Aki’s height when subtracted from Pierre’s height will result to 240 centimeters. How tall is Aki?


Let:

Aki's height = g

Pierre's height = 3g - 4

(3g - 4) - g = 240

Combine like terms

3g - g = 2g

2g - 4 = 240

Transpose 4

2g = 240 + 4

2g = 244

2g/2 = 244/2

g = 122

Aki is 122 cm tall

3g - 4

3(122) - 4

366 - 4 = 362

Pierre is 362 cm tall
