13. 1. Sarah's profit depends on the number pancakes she can sell. which is the independent variable?A.Sarah's profitB.number of pancakes C.number of pancake chicken sellD.All of the above ​


Answer:

b

Step-by-step explanation:

BASTA TAMA YAN PROMISE

Answer:

C. Number of Pancakes She can sell.

Explanation.

Choice A.

Your question literally tells that Sara's profit depends on the number pancakes she can sell. Sara's profit would be a dependent variable.

Choice B.

You question again literally tells that Sara's profit depends on the number of pancakes she can sell. Furthermore, Sara can bake as many pancakes as she wants but she wouldn't make profit if she doesn't sell them.

Choice C.

Sara can make profit out of selling her pancakes in which the more she sells, the more she profits. The number of pancakes she can sell is an independent variable.

Choice D.

Choice A and B, which is above Choice D, wouldn't make an independent variable. This means that not all choices above would make an independent variable.
