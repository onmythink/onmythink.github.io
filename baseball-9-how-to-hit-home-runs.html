29. A baseball player leads off the game and hits along home run. The ball leaves the bat at an angle of 25° with a velocity of 30 m/s. Determine the following:a. How long will it take the ball before it strikes the ground?b. How high will it go?c. At what distance does the ball covers?​


Answer:

Given values:

vi = 30 m/s

θ = 25°

-----------------------------------------------------------

Solution: (a. time of flight)

t = (2vi sin θ) / g

t = [2(30 m/s)(sin 25°)] / 9.81 m/s²

t = 2.58 s

Note: If the value of g used is 9.8 m/s², the answer is 2.59 s.

--------------------------------------------------------

Solution: (b. maximum height)

y max = (vi sin θ)² / (2g)

y max = (30 m/s sin 25°)² / (2 × 9.81 m/s²)

y max = 8.19 m

Note: If the value of g used is 9.8 m/s², the answer is 8.20 m.

--------------------------------------------------------

Solution: (c. range)

R = vi² sin 2θ / g

R = (30 m/s)²[sin (2 × 25°)] / 9.81 m/s²

R = 70.28 m

Note: If the value of g used is 9.8 m/s², the answer is 70.35 m.
