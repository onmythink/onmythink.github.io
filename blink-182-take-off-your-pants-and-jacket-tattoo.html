13. A jacket is on sale for 20% off the regular price. The sale price is $50.00. What is the regular price of the jacket?


Answer: $62.5

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's identify the given values first.

Discount rate: 20%

Sale price: $50.00

Regular price: ?

.

Now, let's solve for the base or the regular price. Let regular price be x.

x - 20%x = 50

(Note: 20% = 0.2)

x - 0.2x = 50

0.8x = 50

[tex]\frac{0.8x}{0.8} = \frac{50}{0.8}[/tex]

x = 62.5

Therefore, the regular price of the jacket is $62.5.

29. You were invited by your cousin to attend his birthday party. you checked your cabinet and there were 3 different jackets, 3 different shirts and 2 different pairs of pants left. by using the fundamental counting principle, how many different outfits consisting of a jacket, shirt and pants are possible?


The answer is 18 possibilities.

Solution:

jackets = 3

shirts = 3

pants = 2

Count combination possibilities:

3 x 3 x 2 = 18

This kind of problem belongs to the Fundamental Counting Principle.

Fundamental C0unting Principle Descripti0n

The Fundamental Counting Principle (also called the counting rule) is a way to determine the number of outcomes in a probability problem. You multiply the events together to get the total number of products. The formula is:

If you have an event "a" and another "b", then all the different outcomes for the events are a x b.

The fundamental counting principle is one of the essential rules in Mathematics, especially in probability problems, and is used to find the number of ways in which the combination of several events can occur.

The fundamental counting principle helps us find the total number of possible outcomes that come from multiple events, whether putting an outfit together with different shirts, pants, and shoes, selecting a meal with other drinks, salad, and entree options, or putting together any group of choices.
