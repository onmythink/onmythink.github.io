10. If you are asked to add up features in Google Sites, What would they be? Why adding such?


Answer:

1. Page Layouts: Allows users to customize the layout of their pages, giving them more control over the look and feel of their content.

2. Content Widgets: Widgets make it easy to embed content from other sources, such as YouTube videos and Google Maps, on your website.

3. Site Search: Allows users to quickly search for content on your website.

4. Google Analytics: Provides insights into how visitors are interacting with your website, such as which pages are getting the most views.

5. Custom URLs: Allows you to create a custom domain name for your website, making it easier to share and promote.

6. Mobile Optimization: Automatically optimizes your website for mobile devices, making it easier for visitors to view your content on the go.

Why these exactly?:

Adding these features to Google Sites allows users to create more engaging and interactive websites with better visibility and usability. With these features, users can create content that is more personalized and tailored to their visitors’ needs, creating a better overall experience for them.

21. The Presentation app included in Google Drive is called? a. Google Docs b. Google Sheets c. Prezi d. Google Slides

Prezi is a short term for “presentation” in Hungarian. It is a presentation software company founded in 2009. Presentations features a map-like overview, unlike a traditional slide based presentation. It allows users to pan between topics, and zoom in on details, and pull back to reveal context.
