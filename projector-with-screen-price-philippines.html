10. 3. Mrs. Dela Cruz is a history teacher. She decided to play a video that shows the heroic life of local heroes of the province of Bulacan. She used LCD projector to play the video. The LCD Projector is located at a table 2 meters away from the white screen. Calculate the illuminance received by the white screen if the LCD projector produces 850 lux? Hint: 1 lux = 1 lm/m²​


Answer:

We can use the inverse square law of light to calculate the illuminance received by the white screen:

Illuminance = (Intensity of light source) / (Distance from source)^2

First, we need to convert the lux unit to lumens per square meter:

850 lux = 850 lm/m²

Next, we can plug in the values:

Illuminance = (850 lm/m²) / (2 m)^2

Illuminance = 212.5 lm/m²

Therefore, the illuminance received by the white screen is 212.5 lumens per square meter.
