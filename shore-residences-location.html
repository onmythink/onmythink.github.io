Explanation:

The philosophy movement o this city started with Thales and his successors,  Anaximander and Anaximenes. Thales' assumption that everything is made of water. His reasoning of what he observed around him made him a philosopher.

Thales' successors expanded and refuted some of his claims. Anaximander agreed that everything is made up of something, but he put forward that matter is made up of something that is not water but instead of what he calls as apeiron. Thales' other successor, Anaximenes, argued that everything is not made of water but of air.

Regardless, the pursuit of knowledge in their desire to explain the composition of the everyday matter around them made these Miletians the first philosophers.

22. if you are residing in one of the locations with a history of earthquakes or near a volcano what are 3 things you need to prepare​


Answer:

      What do we need to bring/prepare before an earthquake or a                                             volcanic eruption happens?

1.  Canned food and Gallons of water

2.  Manual (Non electric) can opener

3.  First Aid Kit

4.  Flashlight and extra batteries

5.  Battery-powered radio/ an operated radio

6.  Sturdy shoes

7.  Respiratory (breathing) protection

          In other words: Mask

8. Eye protection (goggles)

                  What will you do if there is a volcanic eruption?

Listen for emergency information and alerts.

Follow evacuation or shelter orders. If advised to evacuate, then do so early.

Avoid areas downstream of the eruption.

Protect yourself from falling ash.

Do not drive in heavy ash fall.

           Where is the safest place to be during a volcanic eruption?

Avoid low-lying areas, areas downwind of the volcano, and river valleys downstream of the volcano. Debris and ash will be carried by wind and gravity. Stay in areas where you will not be further exposed to volcanic eruption hazards.

                    What will you do if there is an earthquake?

If you are in a car, pull over and stop. Set your parking brake.

If you are in bed, turn face down and cover your head and neck with a pillow.

If you are outdoors, stay outdoors away from buildings.

Do not get in a doorway.

Do not run outside.

                           'And follow the Duck, Cover and Hold!'

               Where is the safest place to be during an earthquake?

The best move is getting under a strong table or desk. If no sturdy object is available, get next to an interior wall with no windows. Finally, HOLD ON to your shelter if you have one, as the temblor will likely involve great shaking. If you have no shelter, hold on to your neck and head with both arms and hands.

29. Kahulugan ng outsourcing,near shoring, off shoring at on shoring


Answer:

Outsourcing, Near shoring, Off-shoring at On-shoring

Ito ay mga terminong gamit sa negosyo, kung saan tinutukoy nito kung saan nangagaling ang manpower ng isang kumpanya. Isa isahin natin ang bawat termino sa mga sumusnod:

Outsourcing:

Ito ay ang pagkuha ng mga mangagawa ng isang kumpanya sa ibang lokasyon kung saan mura ang labor ngunit may kalidad ang gawa. Ito ay gngawa upang makatipid sa halaga ng pasweldo ng mga mangagawa. Madalas ang mga kumpanya na may ganitong business model ay mga kumpanya mula sa Europe at America at ang mga bansang pinagkukunan ng manpower ay China, India, Philippines, Indonesia, Guatemala, Brazil

_

Ang mga sumusunod ay klase ng outsourcing:

Near shoring:

Ito ay pagkuha ng mga mangagawa sa karatig na bansa kung saan ang time difference ay kaunit lamang at ang minimum income ng bansang pinagkunan ay hindi rin gaanong mataas.

Ito ay ideal sa mga kumpanya na kritikal ang palagiang komunikasyon, dahil kakaunti lamang ang time difference.

Off-shoring

Ito ay pagkuha ng mga mangagawa sa mas malayong bansa ngunit higit na mas mura ang minimimum income at way of living. Hindi ito ideal sa mga kumpanya na kritikal ang komuniksayon at pagpupulong dahil na din sa malaking agwat ng oras ng mga bansa.

On-shoring

Ito ay pagkuha ng mga mangagawa sa parehong bansa (gaya ng probinsya)  ngunit higit na mas mura ang way of living. Ito ay kadalasan business model ng mga pabrika kung saan ang main office ay nasa siyudad at ang gawaan o pasilidad ay nasa probinsya kung saan mas mura ang raw materials at labor.
