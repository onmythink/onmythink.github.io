23. the age of derrick is 1/3 that of his father's age and 1/5 of that his grandfather's age if the sum of their ages is 144 how old is derrick? solution


Answer:

16

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = Derrick's age

With this, his father's age is 3x, and his grandfather's age is 5x.

Now, we have the equation:

x + 3x + 5x = 144

Solving that,

9x = 144 ---> x = 16

Therefore, Derrick is 16 years old.

Answer:

(d.) 16

Derrick is 16 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Their ages:

Derrick: xFather's age: 3x  (Father is 3 times Derricks age or Derrick's age is 1/3 his father's age)Grandfather's age:  5x  (Grandfather is 5 times Derrick's age or Derricks age is 1/5 his grandfather's age)Sum of their ages:  144

Add their ages to solve for x, Derrick's age:

x + 3x + 5x = 144

9x = 144

9x/9 = 144/9

x = 16

Derrick is 16 years old.

25. Ano ang Battle of the Bulge


Ang Labanan ng Bulso, na kilala rin bilang Ardennes Counteroffensive, ay ang huling pangunahing kampanya na nakakasakit sa Aleman sa Western Front sa panahon ng Ikalawang Digmaang Pandaigdig, at naganap mula 16 Disyembre 1944 hanggang 25 Enero 1945. Inilunsad ito sa pamamagitan ng malawak na halamang Ardennes na rehiyon ng Wallonia sa silangang Belgium, hilagang Pransya, at Luxembourg, sa pagtatapos ng digmaan sa Europa. 29. Monasterio de san millan de la cogolla wikipedia


El Monasterio de San Millán de la Cogolla es una institución religiosa fundada en el siglo VI d.C. por San Millán, situada en el municipio de San Millán de la Cogolla en la comunidad autónoma de La Rioja. Está formado por la comunidad de monjes que continúan la tradición iniciada por el eremita Millán. Desde el siglo VI hasta nuestros días se han dado distintos tipos de vida monástica: eremitas, cenobio, monasterio visigodo dúplice con regla hispánica, monasterio mozárabe, monasterio benedictino, y en la actualidad convento de frailes de la Orden de Agustinos Recoletos.
