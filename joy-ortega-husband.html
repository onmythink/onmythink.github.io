She will repay it by making monthly payments of P10,000 per month for the next 2 years at an interest rate of 6% per year compounded monthly. How much did Hazel Joy borrow?​


Answer:

P = 2,469.79

Step-by-step explanation:

Use this formula for present value

=>

P= F/ (1+j)^n

F= P10,000

j= 6% or 0.06

n= 24

t= 2

Compounded monthly = 12

formula of n = m x t

P= P10,000/ (1+0.06)^24

Answer: P2,469.79

[Low level of physical fitness in Spanish adolescents. Relevance for future cardiovascular health (AVENA study)]

[Article in Spanish]

Francisco B Ortega et al. Rev Esp Cardiol. 2005 Aug.

Explanation:

Several studies have demonstrated that physical fitness in childhood and adolescence is related to cardiovascular risk in adulthood. Current data on the physical fitness of Spanish adolescents are not available.
