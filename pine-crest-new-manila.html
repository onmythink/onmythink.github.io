Logan genetically engineered a new type of fir tree and a new type of pine tree.  

set variables

Let F= height of fir treeP= height of pine tree

The combined height of one fir tree and one pine tree is  21 meters.

F + P = 21m            Equation 1

The height of  4 fir trees stacked on top of each other is  24 meters taller than one pine tree.  

4F = 24 + P             Equation 2

How tall are the types of trees that Logan genetically engineered?

To solve, transpose equation one such that there is an isolated variable

P = 21 - F                Equation 1, transposed

Incorporate Equation 1 to Equation 2

4F= 24 + (21 - F)4F = 45 - F4F + F = 455F = 45F = 9m = height of Fir Tree  

To get height of pine tree, use value of height of fir tree and transposed equation 1

P = 21 - 9 = 12m = height of Pine Tree

11. the pines beetle laying eggs in the pine tree what is the relationship​


The relationship between the pine beetle and the pine tree is an example of a symbiotic relationship. The beetle lays its eggs in the pine tree, and the hatched larvae feed on the inner bark of the tree, providing nutrients for the beetle. In return, the beetle helps to keep the tree healthy by removing dead bark and creating tunnels in the tree for airflow. This helps the tree to breathe and prevents disease from taking hold.

25. what is the reproductive structures of PinesWhat is the reproductive structures of Pines​


The familiar pinecone is the reproductive structure of the pine tree. But pine trees actually produce two kinds of cones: a female cone and a male cone. Cones are modified stems that have been retasked for reproduction.

Pine trees reproduce by producing seeds. Unlike deciduous trees, which produce seeds that are surrounded by fruit, pine seeds are located on scales of structures called cones (pine cones). Pine trees possess both male and female reproductive structures, or cones. Both male and female cones are on the same tree
