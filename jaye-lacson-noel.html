26. Maria was born 6 years before Noel. In 2000, Maria's age was 2 years more than 4 years the age of Noel. How old is Noel in 2010?with solution pls​


Answer:

Let's start by setting up some equations based on the information given:

1) Maria's age in 2000: M

2) Noel's age in 2000: N (=M-6)

3) Equating Maria's age to 4 years more than 2 years of Noel's age: M = 2 + 4(N-2)

We want to find Noel's age in 2010, so we need to calculate how much he would've aged from 2000 to 2010. That's a difference of 10 years, so we can add 10 to his age in 2000 to find his age in 2010:

Noel's age in 2010: N + 10

To solve for Noel's age, we'll substitute the expression for M in terms of N from equation 3 into equation 1:

M = 2 + 4(N-2)

M = 4N - 6

N - 6 = 4(N - 2) - 6

N - 6 = 4N - 14

3N = 8

N = 8/3

This tells us that Noel was 8/3 years old (or 2 years and 4 months) in 2000. To find his age in 2010, we add 10 years:

Noel's age in 2010: 8/3 + 10 = 38/3 years old (or 12 years and 2 months old)

Therefore, Noel was 12 years and 2 months old in 2010.

30. Noel sold a lot of land for P550,000 and was given a commission of 7.5%. How much did Noelreceive as commission?​


Answer:

₱41,250.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Commission is easily calculated, as you only need two parts: total value of sales generated, and the commission percentage that the seller takes home. All you do is multiply the two numbers.

Formula:

Commission = Total Sales x Commission Percentage

₱550,000 - Total Sales

7.5% - Commission Percentage

₱550,000 x 7.5% = ₱41,250.00
