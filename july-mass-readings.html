26. Additional Activities A. Read and solve the problem using the 4-Step Plan. Julie makes 200 regular paces as he walks around a a quare park. If one pace of Julie meaures 25cm. What is the length of one side of the park?​


Answer:

=12.5m

Step-by-step explanation:

Julie makes 200 regular paces as he walks around a square park. One pace of Julie meaures 25cm.

Therefore circumference of park = 200 × 25

Circumference = 5000 cm

Circumference of a square = 4 x length of one side.

Therefore, length of one side = 5000/4 = 1250 cm.

=12.5 m

Ambitious: They are competitive and have a high need for achievement.
