25. Nadia invested 65000 at 14% simple interest for 6 years How much interest will be earned


SIMPLE INTEREST

==================================

Problem

Nadia invested 65000 at 14% simple interest for 6 years How much interest will be earned?

Solution

We can solve this problem in which where p is equal to principal, r equal to rate and t is equal to time in the formula of simple interest.

[tex]\bold{Formula\:||\:S_i = PRT}[/tex][tex]\large\tt{\red{S_{Interest}} = 65,000(0.14) (6) }[/tex]

Multiply p and r by t to get the unknown interest. transform the percent of rate into decimal form.

[tex]\large\tt{S_{Interest} = PRT}[/tex]

[tex]\large\tt{S_{Interest} = 65,000(\frac{14\%}{100}) (6) }[/tex]

[tex]\large\tt{S_{Interest} = 65,000(0.14) (6) }[/tex]

[tex]\large\tt{S_{Interest} = 65,000(0.84) }[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\large\tt\red{54,600.00 = S_{Interest}} }[/tex]

Final Answer

Therefore, Nadia will earn Php 54,600.00 at simple interest after 6 years.
