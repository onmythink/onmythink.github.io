17. The total cost for a bucket of popcorn and 4 movie tickets is $56. The total cost for the same size bucket of popcorn and 6 movie tickets is $80. The cost of a bucket of popcorn is $8. Which equation represents the relationship between y, the total cost of the popcorn and movie tickets, and x, the number of movie tickets that are purchased?


The relationship of Equation
Let y be the cost of bucket of popcorn.
Let x be the number of movie tickets that are purchased.

y + 4x = 56
y + 6x = 80

The above equation represent the relationship between y, the total cost of the popcorn and movie tickets, and x, the number of movie tickets that are purchased.

Finding x
-1(y + 4x = 56)

y + 6x = 80

-y - 4x = 56
y + 6x = 80
2x = 24
x = 12

Finding y
y + 4(12) = 56
y + 48 = 56
y = 56 - 48
y = 8
