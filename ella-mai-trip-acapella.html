1. Ella made a trip of 108 kilometers in 2¼ hours.What was her average speed?​


Answer:

48 kph

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the average speed, we should use the formula:

s = d/t

the given distance is 108 km and the given time is 2 1/4 hours. So,

s = 108/2.25

( I converted the fraction ( 2 1/4 ) into decimal first )...

So, the average speed is 48 kph.

3. texture of acapella-


TEXTURE OF ACAPELLA.

ANSWER:

polyphonic

Explanation:

Rounds, canons, and fugues are all polyphonic. (Even if there is only one melody, if different people are singing or playing it at different times, the parts sound independent.)

THEREFORE, THE TEXTURE OF ACAPELLA IS POLYPHONIC.
