To compute the total cost of 12 watches at a price of 24,000.00 each, you can multiply the number of watches by the price of each watch.

Here's the calculation:

12 (number of watches) x 24,000.00 (price of each watch) = 288,000.00

So the total cost of the 12 watches is 288,000.00

And to compute the difference of the price of 12 watches and 735,000.00 you can substract 735,000.00 to 288,000.00

Here's the calculation:

735,000.00 - 288,000.00 = 447,000.00

So the difference of the price between 12 watches and 735,000.00 is 447,000.00
