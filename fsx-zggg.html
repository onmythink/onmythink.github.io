The mark up rate is 30%.

Mark up rate = (Selling Price / Cost - 1) x 100
                     = (3250/2500 - 1) x 100
                     = (1.3-1) x 100
                     = 30 %
Mark on rate based on cost = ((3250-2500)/2500) x 100% = 30% 
Mark on rate based on selling price = ((3250-2500)/3250) x 100% = 23.08%
