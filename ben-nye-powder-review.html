Given:

Length - 8 cmWidth - 7 cmHeight - 3 cm

Answer & Solution:

[tex]\large\rm V=lwh[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm V=(8 \: cm)(7 \: cm)(3 \: cm)[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm V=(56 \: cm {}^{2} )(3 \: cm)[/tex]

[tex]\large\rm V=168 \: cm {}^{3} [/tex]

Hence, the volume of each soup is 168 cm³
