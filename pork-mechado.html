Answer:

Paano nagsimula ang mechado?

Ang Mechado ay isang putahe ng mga Pilipino na nagsimula noong panahon ng mga Kastila. Ang pangunahing sangkap ng Mechado ay karne ng baka na niluluto ng mahabang oras sa tomato sauce kasama ang mga gulay tulad ng patatas, karot, at bell pepper.

Tuwing kailan hinahanda ang mechado?

Ang paghahanda ng Mechado ay maaaring gawin sa anumang araw. Ito ay karaniwang handa sa mga espesyal na okasyon tulad ng mga selebrasyon o pista.

Meron pa bang mechado?

Oo, mayroon pa ring Mechado at patuloy itong nagiging sikat bilang isang popular na putahe sa mga Pilipino.

Ano ang mga sangkap ?

Ang mga pangunahing sangkap ng Mechado ay karne ng baka, tomatong gata, sibuyas, bawang, toyo, asin, paminta, patatas, karot, bell pepper, at iba pang mga gulay na maaaring isama sa recipe.

5. 4. Jay bought pork for his pork chop menu. The pork in the supermarket costs P250.50. A 2% sales tax was added to it. How much was the sales tax? How much is the buying price of the pork? Show the sulution​


Answer:

Sales Tax : The sales tax of the pork was Php 5.01.

Pork Price : The price of the pork was Php 255.51.

Solution:

Sales Tax Solution:

Convert 2% to decimal like the procedure below.

2% ÷ 100 = 0.02

Then multiply 250.50 by 0.02 like the procedure below.

250.50 x 0.02 = 5.01

Sales Tax : Php 5.01

Pork Price Solution:

Add 250.50 and 5.01 like the procedure below.

250.50 + 5.01 = 255.51

Pork Price : Php 255.51

12. tom had cuts of pork. pork 1 is 2 1/4kg; and pork 2 is 3 3/4kg. how heavy are the cuts of pork?​


Problem:Tom had cuts of pork. pork 1 is 2 1/4kg; and pork 2 is 3 3/4kg. how heavy are the cuts of pork?​

Solution:

Total weight of pork = weight of pork 1 + weight of pork 2

Total weight of pork = 2 1/4kg + 3 3/4kg

Total weight of pork = (2 + 3)(1/4 + 3/4)

Total weight of pork = 5 + 4/4

Total weight of pork = 5 + 1

Total weight of pork = 6kg
