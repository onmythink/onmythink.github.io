If the distance between two points on a map is directly proportional to the actual distance between the locations, how far is it from village A and village C in kilometers?


Using the idea of ratio and proportion you'll have the distance from A to C as 
[tex] \frac{AC}{9cm} = \frac{20km}{6cm} [/tex]
[tex]AC = \frac{(20km)(9cm)}{6cm} [/tex]
cm will cancel out
AC = 30km

18. 10.A rectangular shaped village has a perimeter of 12km.If the population density is 2100 people per square km and width of the village is 2km, calculate the approximate population of the village.


Perimeter = 2(length) + 2(width)

Let x be the length, solve for length:

12 = 2x + 2(2)

12 - 4 = 2x

2x = 8

2x/2 = 8/2

x = 4  

length = 4 km

Find the area of the village:

Area = length  ×  width

Area = (4 km) (2 km)

Area = 8 km²

Find the approximate population of the village given the population density:

2,100 people/square km   × 8 square km = 16,800 people

Answer:  16,800 people
