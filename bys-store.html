4. what are the examples of subjects, subject complement, subject of a preposition, direct object and apposite of cooking,bying,asking and waiting ​


Here are the examples of subjects, subject complement, subject of a preposition, direct object, and appositive of the verbs cooking, buying, asking, and waiting:

1. Cooking:

- Subject: She

- Subject complement: is a great cook.

- Direct object: She is cooking spaghetti for dinner.

- Appositive: My friend, the chef, is cooking dinner tonight.

2. Buying:

- Subject: He

- Subject complement: is a savvy shopper.

- Subject of a preposition: He went to the store for buying groceries.

- Direct object: He is buying a new phone.

- Appositive: My brother, a shopaholic, is buying clothes again.

3. Asking:

- Subject: They

- Subject complement: are curious.

- Subject of a preposition: They are asking for directions.

- Direct object: They are asking the teacher a question.

- Appositive: My neighbor, the journalist, is asking for an interview.

4. Waiting:

- Subject: We

- Subject complement: are patient.

- Subject of a preposition: We are waiting at the bus stop.

- Direct object: We are waiting for the movie to start.

- Appositive: My sister, the actress, is waiting for her big break.

Let, the whole amount be 5/5 or 1 whole,

And let the remaining part of money be ₹x,

=> 1/5 + 2/5 + x = 1

=> 3/5 + x = 1

=> x = 1 - 3/5

=> x = 5/5 - 3/5

Therefore, x = 2/5
