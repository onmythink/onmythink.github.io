12. Lily printed a tarpaulin for the birthday of her sister. Its size is 384 in. and the price per square foot is Php 15.00. How much did Lily pay for the tarpaulin? step by step solving;​


Answer:

To solve this problem, we need to first convert the size of the tarpaulin from inches to square feet.

Convert inches to feet:

384 inches ÷ 12 inches/foot = 32 feet

Calculate the area in square feet:

Area = Length x Width

Area = 32 feet x (Width unknown)

Since we are given the size of the tarpaulin, we can assume that the width is also 1 foot. Therefore:

Area = 32 feet x 1 foot = 32 square feet

Calculate the total cost:

Cost = Area x Price per square foot

Cost = 32 square feet x Php 15.00/square foot

Cost = Php 480.00

Therefore, Lily paid Php 480.00 for the tarpaulin.

15. 2. Mrs. De Vera asked you to order a tarpaulin whose perimeter is 10x2 - 2y .If thewidth of the tarpaulin is -2x2 + 3y. What is the length of tarpaulin?​


Answer:

7x² - 4y

Step-by-step explanation:

P = 2L + 2W

10x²-2y = 2L + 2(-2x² + 3y)

10x²-2y = 2L - 4x² + 6y

2L = 10x² - 2y + 4x² - 6y = 14x²-8y

L = (14x²-8y) / 2 = 7x² - 4y
