2. what are the industrial history of municipality of polomolok south cotabato? ​


Answer:

The town of Polomolok is located at the southern portion of South Cotabato and is about 300 meters above sea level at the base of the majestic Mt. Matutum. The name Polomolok was derived from the B’laan term “FLOMLOK’ which means hunting grounds. In the early years, the place where the Poblacion is presently situated was known for its abundant wild life. There were no roads, so the lowlanders befriended the B’laan highlanders and utilized them as guide.
