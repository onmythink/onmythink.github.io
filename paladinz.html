2. PALADIN CASTS TWO BLESSINGS OF WISDOM ON YOUNG DRAGONHAWK. YOUNG DRAGONHAWK ATTACKS TWICE. HOW MANY CARDS PALADIN WILL DRAW?


In the game by game giant Blizzard, Hearthstone, when the hero Paladin casts two Blessings of Wisdom on his or her minion Young Dragonhawk, then the Young Dragonhawk attacks twice, the hero Paladin will draw two cards. This is because when the spell Blessings of Wisdom is cast on a minion, the hero can draw one card per attack made.

If you want to have the Blessing of Wisdom spell card, it can be obtained by the following ways:

1. buying a Classic card pack in the game Shop

2. through crafting, or

3. as a reward in the Game mode Arena.
