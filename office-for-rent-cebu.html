Agreed to share office space with another local agency, Musiko FM. La Vien Music agreed to pay one-fourth of the rent, a portion of the office receptionist’s salary, and one-fourth of the utility bills. Paid ₱40,000 for the rent of the office

It seems like La Vien Music has agreed to share office space with Musiko FM, and has agreed to pay one-fourth of the rent, a portion of the office receptionist's salary, and one-fourth of the utility bills. The payment for the rent of the office space is ₱40,000.

Assuming that the agreement is for one month, here's how the payment breakdown might look like:

Rent:

La Vien Music's share = 1/4 x ₱40,000 = ₱10,000

Office Receptionist's Salary:

The amount La Vien Music needs to contribute towards the receptionist's salary is not mentioned. However, assuming it is also split equally between the two agencies, and the total salary cost is ₱15,000, then La Vien Music's share would be 1/2 x ₱15,000 = ₱7,500.

Utility Bills:

La Vien Music's share of the utility bills would also be 1/4 of the total amount. Assuming the total utility bills for the month is ₱5,000, then La Vien Music's share would be 1/4 x ₱5,000 = ₱1,250.

Therefore, the total amount that La Vien Music would need to pay for one month would be:

Rent + Office Receptionist's Salary + Utility Bills

= ₱10,000 + ₱7,500 + ₱1,250

= ₱18,750

This calculation is based on the assumption that the agreement is for one month, and that the office receptionist's salary is also split equally between the two agencies. However, if there are other details that are not mentioned or different terms in the agreement, the payment breakdown may vary.
