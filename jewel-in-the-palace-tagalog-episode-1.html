30. the ratio of the oatmeal cookies to the chocolate chip cookie that jewel baked is 2:1. After jewel gave 42 oatmeal cookies to her friends, the ratio became 3:5. How many cookies did jewel have at first?


Let:
x = no. of oatmeal cookies
y = no. of chocolate chip cookies

x : y = 2 : 1 (multiply the means and extremes)
x = 2y
y = x/2 --> Eq1


2nd condition:

(x - 42) : y = 3 : 5 (multiply the means and extremes)
5(x- 42) = 3y
5x - 210 = 3y
y = (5x - 210)/3 --> Eq2


Eq1 = Eq2 (y=y)

x/2 = (5x - 210)/3 (cross multpily)
3x = 2(5x - 210)
3x = 10x - 420
7x = 420
x = 60


Solve for y ( use Eq1):

y = x/2
y = 60/2
y = 30

Answer:
Jewel had 90 cookies at first.
(60 oatmeal cookies and 30 chocolate chip cookies)
