21. When a person is 50 kg in mass, what is its weight?A. 50 kgB. 490 kgC. 50 NewtonD. 490 Newton​


Answer:

D. 490 N

Step-by-step explanation:

On earth, 50 kg translates to 492 Newtons.

50 kg · 9.81 m/s² = 492 kg·m/s²

1 kg·m/s² is the same as 1 Newton. A Newton is a unit of force. So is a pound. And 492 N is approximately 111 lbs.

492 N · 0.225 N/lbs = 110.7 lbs
