17. what are the metallic / non metallic deposits that can be found in the philippines?

The non-metallic deposits that can be found in the Philippines are limestone, marble, clay, sand, and gravel. Also, other quarry materials.

22. metallic minerals in the philippines and their location?


The Philippines is considered to be the fifth most mineral-rich country in the world, especially in terms of gold, copper, chromite and nickel. This can be attributed to its location, which is within the Pacific Ring of Fire - the home of about 75% of the world's active volcanoes.

Here are the metallic minerals in the Philippines and some of their locations:

Iron: Ilocos Norte, Camarines Norte, Cotabato, Nueva Vizcaya, Surigao, Cagayan Valley, Marinduque, Zamboanga, Samar, Bulacan, Davao

Nickel: Surigao del Norte, Davao Oriental, Palawan, Romblon, Samar, Camarines Norte, Occidental Mindoro

Gold and Silver: Benguet, Camarines Norte, Masbate, Bulacan, Surigao

Copper: Surigao del Norte

Chromite: Northern and Southern Luzon, Zambales

Platinum: Zambales, Cebu, Samar
