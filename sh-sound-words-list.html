16. identify final consonant clusters form words with final consonant cluster read the following words.pay close attention to the final sound, th, sh, ck, sk, sp​


Answer:

Here are some words with final consonant clusters:

Bath - th

Fish - sh

Sack - ck

Desk - sk

Clasp - sp

Mask - sk

Help - lp

Jump - mp

Last - st

West - st

Final consonant clusters are two or more consonant sounds that appear at the end of a word. These clusters can be made up of different consonant sounds, such as th, sh, ck, sk, and sp. Knowing these clusters can help with spelling and pronunciation of words.
