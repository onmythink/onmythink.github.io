A. ¼ kg spaghetti

¾ cup (125g) bacon, chopped

6 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup water

1 pack NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream 250ml

4 tbsp grated cheese

1 sachet MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®

STEP 2

Cook bacon in a pan to render fat. Strain bacon bits and set aside. (5 minutes)
