12. what is the difference between side cutters and flush cutters​


Answer:

The term "flush" means level or straight and on the same plane, so flush cutters cut a wire level. Side cutters, or angle cutters, cut on an angle, meaning the wire edge will be cut to one side.

30. It plots a design or graph on a sheet of paper spread and fixed over a rectangular flatbed table.A. Line PrinterB. Drum PlotterC. Flatbed Plotter​


Answer:

C. Flatbed Plotter

Explanation:

Flatbed plotter plots a design or graph on a sheet of paper that is spread and fixed over a rectangular flatbed table. In this type of plotter, normally the paper does not move and the pen holding mechanism is designed to provide all types of motions necessary to draw complex designs and graphs.
