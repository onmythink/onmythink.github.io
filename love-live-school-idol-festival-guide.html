13. example of idols of the theater​ and idols of the market place


Explanation:

In Francis Bacon's “The Four Idols”, Bacon describes to us the Four Idols which are the idols of the tribe, the idols of the cave, the idols of the marketplace, and the idols of the theater. According to Bacon, these four idols prevent us from progressing in scientific investigation.

Idola theatri (singular Idolum theatri) is a type of tendency towards logical fallacy or error, normally translated as "idols of the theatre". The Latin was coined by Sir Francis Bacon in his Novum Organum—one of the earliest treatises arguing the case for the logic and method of modern science.

23. Why do Filipinos love the k-pop idols, fashion, Food, drama, boyband or girl group? ​


Answer:

From K-pop to K-dramas, Filipinos have loved Korean pop culture since the late 1990s when the so-called Hallyu wave started to gain ground around the world.

According to the Korea Foundation, the Philippines has the highest growth rate of hallyu fans among 113 countries in 2018.

The Hallyu wave is a phrase used to describe the distribution and spread of South Korean cultural products such as food, music and television series to the rest of Asia and to other parts of the world.
