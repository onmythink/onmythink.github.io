21. Explain the EO 292, section 3, provided for by the civil service commission?​


Answer:

Executive Order No. 292, also known as the Administrative Code of 1987, is a document issued by the President of the Philippines that outlines the organization and structure of the national government of the Philippines. Section 3 of the Executive Order provides for the establishment of the Civil Service Commission (CSC), which is responsible for ensuring the efficient and effective functioning of the civil service. The CSC has the power to administer and enforce laws, rules, and regulations related to the civil service, including matters related to appointments, promotions, transfers, and disciplinary action. Additionally, the CSC is responsible for conducting examinations for positions in the civil service and for maintaining a register of eligible candidates for appointment to those positions.

The civil service exam is an exam that tests people applying for specific civil service jobs. The point of the test is to prove applicants have the appropriate skills and are eligibile for a specific position. The exam varies between different fields and levels of government.
