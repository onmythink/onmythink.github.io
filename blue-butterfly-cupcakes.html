14. Among a species of butterflies, the color blue (B) is a dominant trait. Yellow color (b) is a recessive trait. If a pure blue butterfly (BB) mates with a pure yellow (bb), what will the offspring look like?


Answer:

All blue

Explanation:

In order to determine what the offspring will look or like or its phenotype, we must first get the possible genotypes by means of a Punnett square.

        b            b

B     Bb         Bb

B     Bb         Bb

Since the genotype of all offspring is Bb, having a dominant trait B indicates that the phenotype is blue. Therefore, all the offspring will be blue.

17. A vanilla cupcake costs twice as much as mocha cupcake. A vanilla and a mocha cupcake cost Php 73.50. How much is 3 mocha and 5 vanilla cupcake​


Vanilla cupcake= 2x
Mocha cupcake = x
Vanilla & mocha cupcake = Php73.50

To get the price of each cupcake, this will be the equation;
2x+x= 73.50

Solution
2x+x= 73.50
3x= 73.50

You need to divide 73.50 by 3 to get the price of the mocha cupcake .
73.50/3
= php24.50

The price of the mocha cupcake is Php24.50 so the vanilla cupcake will be Php49 because the price of it is twice the price of the mocha.

The question is how much is 3 mocha and 5 vanilla, so this will be the equation;

(3*Php24.50)+(5*Php49)
(‘*’ represents multiply)

Solution
3*24.50= 73.50
5*49=245
Php73.50 + Php245
= Php318.50

Answer
Php 318.50

28. anita asks her mother to prepare cupcakes for her 25 classmates .if there are 100 cupcakes,how many cupcakes,how many cupcakes will each classmate receive?


Answer: 4 Cupcakes per person

Explanation:

we must Define the Following: A.G.O.N.S.A Method

In AGONSA, we asked:

What are Asked? (Ano ang Tinatanong?)

What are the Given? (Ano ang mga clue?)

What Operation should be use? (Kailangan ba magdagdag, magbawas, paramihin o hatiin?)

What are the Numbers? (Ano ang mga numerong Nakikita?)

What is the Solution? (Ano ang mga hakbang sa pagsagot nito?)

What is the Answer? (Ano ang nakuhang kasagutan?)

Asked - How many cupcakes will each classmate receive?

Given - 25 classmates & 100 cupcakes

O -   'will each classmate receive', the operation will be use is Division (÷)

N - 100 ÷ 25

S - In the Image:

A = 4 Cupcakes per person
