28. what is the denonative of rainbow​


Answer:

The denotative meaning of rainbow is "a curved band of colors, formed in the sky by the refraction and dispersion of the sun's rays in rain or other water droplets in the atmosphere, that exhibits red on the outer part and violet on the inner side." This is the dictionary definition of the word and refers to the physical phenomenon of a rainbow that can be observed in the sky after a rainstorm.
