13. The total cost for a bucket of popcorn and 4 movie tickets is $56. The total cost for the same size bucket of popcorn and 6 movie tickets is $80. The cost of a bucket of popcorn is $8. Which equation represents the relationship between y, the total cost of the popcorn and movie tickets, and x, the number of movie tickets that are purchased?


The relationship of Equation
Let y be the cost of bucket of popcorn.
Let x be the number of movie tickets that are purchased.

y + 4x = 56
y + 6x = 80

The above equation represent the relationship between y, the total cost of the popcorn and movie tickets, and x, the number of movie tickets that are purchased.

Finding x
-1(y + 4x = 56)

y + 6x = 80

-y - 4x = 56
y + 6x = 80
2x = 24
x = 12

Finding y
y + 4(12) = 56
y + 48 = 56
y = 56 - 48
y = 8

24. A regular Coke costs ₱ 18 while a large Coke costs twice the price of a regular Coke. Michelle sold a total of 25 cups of regular and large Coke. The revenue for the soda sold was ₱ 702. How many cups of large Coke were sold?


Answer:

14 cups

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us represent the number of regular Cokes sold as R, and the number of large Cokes sold as L. Since larges cost twice the regular, they cost 2 * ₱18 or ₱36.

From the given, we can make the following equations:

R + L = 25 ... {1}

18R + 36L = 702 ... {2}

We can use the method of elimination,

{2} - 18 {1}

18R + 36L = 702

-18R - 18L = -450

         18L = 252

            L = 14

Therefore, 14 cups of large Coke were sold.
