The correct answer among the listed given choices is letter b. Prodromal stage is the stage of infection that is Aliya experiencing having a slight fever because she visited a friend in the hospital a few days ago.

4 Stages of most Infectious Disease Incubation Stage - is the stage after the initial entry of the pathogen into the host (patient). During this stage, the patient is unaware that a disease is beginning to develop.Prodromal stage - is the stage after incubation stage. It is the host begins to experience general signs and symptoms of illness such as pain, fever or soreness.Clinical stage - this is the stage which the signs and symptoms of the disease are most obvious and severe.Convalescence stage- is the stage of declining of infections and recovery period to normal functions of the body.

Chain of infectionPathogen - it is the infectious agents that cause diseasesReservoir - it is a place in the environment that germs/ pathogen livesMode of Exit - it is the way the infectious agent leaves the reservoir Mode of Transmission- it is the way the infectious agent can be passed onMode of Entry- it is the way the infectious agent can enter a new host Susceptible Host- any person that can be infected

20. Almira types 50 pages in 2 hours. How long will it take her to type 80 pages?


Answer:

3 1/5 or 3.2 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Let n be the missing number

50:2=80:n

50n=(80)(2)

50n=160

divide both side by 50 to find the missing number n

50n/50=160/50

n= 3 1/5 hours or 3.2 hours
