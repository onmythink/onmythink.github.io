Every Month the Credit Card Company charges you for Php 1,531.06.

Your total balance after that is  Php 42, 213.47

Answer:

Credit Card Fee = 3.5%

Find the fee:

Fee = 3.5% of 43,744.53 pesos

Fee = 0.035 x 43,744.53

Fee = 1531.06

Find the balance owed:

Credit Card balance = 43,744.53 pesos

Fee = 1531.06 pesos

Total = 43,744.53 + 1531.06

Total = 45275.59 pesos

24. Thieves can capture a credit card number by placing a(n) ______ on an atm.


Answer:

SKIMMER

Explanation:

An ATM skimmer is a malicious device that thieves attach to ATMs in order to steal your card information. Usually it’s a magnetic stripe reader stuck on top of or inside the default card reader. It’s frequently paired with a device that retrieves your card’s PIN number like a tiny video camera hidden near the ATM or a fake PIN pad attached on top of the original.
