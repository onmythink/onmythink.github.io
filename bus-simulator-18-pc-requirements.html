Rose = [tex] \frac{18pcs}{4 days} [/tex]
Andrea = [tex] \frac{22pcs}{2 days} [/tex]

Multiply Andrea's rate by 2:
([tex] \frac{22pcs}{2 days} [/tex]) · 4 = [tex] \frac{44pcs}{4days} [/tex]
Andrea can make 44pcs in 4 days.

44 - 18 = 26

Andrea can make 26 more pcs of embroidery than Rose in 4 days.
