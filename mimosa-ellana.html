1. bashful mimosa is a​


Answer:

Mimosa pudica or Bashful mimosa  (from Latin: pudica "shy, bashful or shrinking"; also called sensitive plant, sleepy plant, action plant, touch-me-not, shameplant is a creeping annual or perennial flowering plant of the pea/legume family Fabaceae and Magnoliopsida taxon, often grown for its curiosity value: the compound leaves fold inward and droop when touched or shaken, defending themselves from harm, and re-open a few minutes later.

22. 1. Mimosa’s square lot has a length of (5m-10). Solve for the area of the lot. 2. Megicula’s rectangular garden has a length of (2n-4) and a width of (2n+4). Solve for the area of the rectangular garden.


AREA

The area of the square lot is 25m²-100m+100.

The area of the rectangular garden is 4n²-16.

Solution:

Area of the square lot
A= s² is the formula in finding the area of the square.
A= (5m-10)²
A= 25m²-50m-50m+100
A= 25m²-100m+ 100.

Area of the rectangular garden
A= LW is the formula for finding the area of the rectangle.
A= (2n-4)(2n+4)
A= 4n²+8n-8n-16
A= 4n²-16.

What is an Area?

The area of a two-dimensional figure is the amount of space it occupies. To put it another way, it’s the quantity that counts how many unit squares cover the surface of a closed figure. Square units, which are commonly expressed as square inches, square feet, and so on, are the standard unit of area. The term ‘area’ refers to an open space. The length and width of a form are used to compute its area. The area of an object refers to how much space it takes up. It’s the area that every shape can occupy.

Area of a Square

The number of square units required to fill a square is its area. When computing the area of a square, we simply consider the length of its sides. A= s² is the formula in finding the area of the square.
Example: Find the area of the square with the side measure 24 cm.
A= s²
A= 24²
A= 576 cm²
576 is the area of the square.

Area of a Rectangle

The area of a rectangle is the space it occupies within its four edges or boundaries. The sides of a rectangle define its area. A= LW is the formula in finding the area of the rectangle.
Example: Find the area of the rectangle with the height of 12 cm and the width of 11 cm
A= LW
A= (12)(11)
A= 132
132 cm² is the area of the rectangle.
