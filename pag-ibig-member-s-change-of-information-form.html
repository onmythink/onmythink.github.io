3. Pag ibig members savings hit record P73 B reflection

This is a reflection of the increasing financial security of Philippine workers and their families, thanks to the government's pro-active measures to promote responsible saving and investments. It is also a testament to the effectiveness of the Pag-IBIG Fund in providing an accessible and affordable savings vehicle for Filipinos. The record-high savings also reflects the growing confidence of Filipinos in the Fund's ability to deliver on its promise of secure and rewarding investments.

Answer:

MANILA, Philippines — Member savings collections of the Home Development Mutual Fund, commonly known as Pag-IBIG, reached a record high P73 billion from January to November last year, giving the agency more flexibility to implement its programs.

During the Laging Handa briefing yesterday, Pag-IBIG department manager for public media affairs Jack Jacinto said Pag-IBIG members saved P72.88 billion from January to November last year.

Jacinto said this is the highest recorded savings for any 10-month period as well as full-year figures and is part of Pag-IBIG’s record-high accomplishments last year in terms of collections and loan releases.

This allowed Pag-IBIG to surpass its target of collecting some P70 billion in savings, of which 50 percent will come from its Modified Pag-IBIG 2 Savings (MP2), a voluntary program for its members.

During the period, Pag-IBIG also released a record P104 billion in housing loans that benefited 93,600 members. It effectively closed in on its goal of providing P110 billion in home loans.

Its short-term loans also increased to P50 billion and aided 2.4 million members. Home loan payments also jumped to a record P131 billion.

Pag-IBIG’s total actively contributing members also went up to 15.1 million as of end-November from the 13.5 million in 2021.

Given Pag-IBIG’s record performance in 2022, Jacinto said the agency is in good financial standing even without an increase in the contribution of its members until 2024.

“We can comply with benefits and demands for loans even if we don’t increase our contribution,” Jacinto said.

Nonetheless, Pag-IBIG will still conduct dialogues with labor and employer groups over the coming months to discuss the need to hike contributions.

13. How to find my unprint mdf form of pag ibig?


Answer:

HOW TO FIND THE UNPRINT MDF FORM OF PAGIBIG?  

First things first, let's know what is PAG-IBIG.  

Although it sounds cheesy and romantic as it translates to Love in Filipino, It does not relate at all in romance.

What then is PAG-IBIG?  

PAG-IBIG actually is and acronym stands for:

PAGtutulungan sa Kinabukasan: Ikaw, Bangko, Industria at Gobyerno.

(Very catchy for a government agency Acronym)  

It basically takes care of the following:

It is a national savings program which intends to provide affordable home loans for Filipinos.  Also known as The Home Development Mutual Fund (HDMF) - a government-owned and controlled corporation under the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council

Now that we have know what is Pag-IBIG and what it does, we can now go back to the question of how to find the unprinted MDF form of PAG-IBIG.

Well the if you are already a member and you have no idea about what your MDF number there are 2 ways to retrieve it:

Call the Pag-IBIG customer service hotline: 8-724-4244 tell the operator that you were not able to print your MDF form and have no idea whether what your number is. Go to any Pag-IBIG branch and request for your MDF form.

If you are a new user and  just registering, follow the steps below:

The following steps are required to find the Pag IBIG MDF application form online:

Go to their online member registration website. Select the "View Registration Information" and then click on "Continue". Enter the required information (Last and first name, and Registration Tracking Number). Enter the CAPTCHA code and hit "Proceed". The site will then direct you to a page where you will fill out your personal details.Once all details have been validated by the system it will then proceed to printing your generated MDF form. Go to a Pag-Ibig branch and validate you MDF number.
