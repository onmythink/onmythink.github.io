26. MOVIE REVIEW OF WONDER WOMAN ​


➛Question:

MOVIE REVIEW OF WONDER WOMAN

Answer:

Wonder Woman is a 2017 superhero film based on the DC Comics character of the same name. The film is directed by Patty Jenkins and stars Gal Gadot as the titular character. It is a refreshing take on the superhero genre, as it has a strong female lead and a female director at the helm. The movie is set during World War I, and it tells the story of Diana, Princess of the Amazons, who lives on the secluded island of Themyscira. When a pilot crashes on the island and tells Diana about the ongoing war, she decides to leave her home to help end the conflict. Along with Steve Trevor (played by Chris Pine), Diana embarks on a journey to find and defeat the villainous Ares, the god of war. One of the best things about Wonder Woman is the way it balances action and emotion. The fight scenes are thrilling and well-executed, but the movie never loses sight of the humanity of its characters. Gal Gadot gives a standout performance as Diana, bringing both strength and vulnerability to the character. Chris Pine is also excellent as Steve Trevor, adding humor and heart to the film.
