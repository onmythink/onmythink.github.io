Maps and globes are very important tools for identifying places on the Earth's surface. Both are representations of the real Earth at a manageable size. A globe is a true representation in miniature. A map is a flat representation of an area, which usually has distortions.

25. The globe does not show all lines of latitude.if you wish to find 50°N,where should you look?


Latitude is the line we can find in the globe that runs parallel from east to west direction. The Latitude line starts at 0° where we find the equatorial region. Above the equatorial region is the northern hemisphere or northern latitude. Below the the equatorial region is the southern hemisphere or the southern latitude. You can locate 15°N, 60°N on the northern hemisphere above the equatorial region. You can locate 30°S,45°S below the equatorial region or in the southern hemisphere.
