21. One of the most basic skill that a basketball player should possess in terms of speed.


Answer:

teamwork po ang tamang sagot

One of the most basic skills that a basketball player should possess in terms of speed is dribbling. Even though there are many things that a basketball player should posses such as shooting ability and passing skills, being able to dribble is one of the basics. A good basketball player should always try to show his dribbling ability because this is what separates him from the rest. Most people do not realize how important dribbling ability is until they play in a team game and they need to go fast to pass or to receive a pass. As soon as someone tries to block your shot or steal the ball, you have to be able to quickly evade that defender because if you don't, you will not be able to get open passes nor you will be able to score baskets. And even if you are able to avoid the defender, you still have to be able to quickly change direction in order to avoid getting blocked. To me, the ability to dribble is almost like a gift; you don't really need to train your hands or feet to be capable of dribbling, it just comes naturally after playing organized sports all your life.
