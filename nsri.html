26. Function of NSRC and ways of achieving


NSRC Functions

Approving the international standards and the specifications of the necessary devices, equipment, supplies and tools in the field of the Search and Rescue and issuing the necessary resolutions in this regards.

Supervising the preparation of the response capabilities through developing the methodologies and the information and experiences exchange with similar bodies at the international level.

Representing the State in the field of the Search and Rescue in association with the concerned authorities at the national, regional and international level.

Preparing the preparatory and operational plans and creating a database and information systems of Search and Rescue.

Preparing, coordinating and implementing the Search and Rescue drills.

Disseminating the culture of Search and Rescue.
