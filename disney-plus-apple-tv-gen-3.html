1. To find the number of residents who listen to radio only, we can use the formula:

(number of residents who listen to radio) - (number of residents who watch both TV and listen to radio) = number of residents who listen to radio only

So, we have:

270 - 120 = 150

Therefore, the answer is d. 150 residents listen to radio only.

2. To find the total number of residents in the community, we can use the formula:

(total number of residents) = (number of residents who watch Netflix) + (number of residents who watch Disney Plus) - (number of residents who watch both Netflix and Disney Plus)

So, we have:

(total number of residents) = 300 + 270 - 120 = 450

Therefore, the answer is b. 450 residents in the community.
