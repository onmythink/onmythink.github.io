Answer:

If the train is set to arrive at Naiga at 10:30 am on Tuesday and 45 minutes, we can subtract the travel time from the arrival time to find the departure time from Divisoria.

The travel time is from Monday late afternoon to Tuesday 10:30 am and 45 minutes, which is approximately 16 hours and 45 minutes.

So, if we subtract 16 hours and 45 minutes from the arrival time, we get the departure time from Divisoria:

10:30 am - 16 hours - 45 minutes = 5:45 pm, Monday afternoon

Therefore, the train should leave Divisoria at 5:45 pm on Monday afternoon to arrive at Naiga at 10:30 am on Tuesday and 45 minutes.
