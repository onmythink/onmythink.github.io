4. In your own words, why is it that it is significantly important to know the profile of our client/s and their respective market?


Answer:

The first reason why its important to know our clients, is because, as a marketing team we as a whole have an understanding the client and what they actually want to achieve. This knowledge is vital as we develop our market and our marketing, to ensure the best outcomes for our clients.

The market profile of a client is key and should be included with all other aspects of their strategy in order to ensure they are able to reach the targeted/desired market. For example, a client that is a school may have a clear understanding of the demographics of parents and the importance of their children/students attending school. It is not only the demographics that are important, but also the profile. For example, this may include the average income of parents in the area, the importance of the local school, and the number of local schools available to the children/students.

Write your answers on the space provided. 1. Will this product make me beautiful/handsome? 2. Will the teenage boys and girls like this product? 3. Will the LGBTQ community be offended by these products? 4. Will this product improve the living condition of its buyer? 5. Will consumers buy these items for their kids?​


AnswerFirst, let us define what Behavioral Profile and Demographic Profile are.Behavioral Profile - or Customer Behavior is the data to understand how clients interact with the product and the company. Consumer behaviors are affected by psychological, personal, social, and geographical factors.Demographic Profile - are the socioeconomic information of a population which includes age, race, location, sex/gender, education, income, marriage rates, birth and death rates, and others.So, for the answers:

1. Will this product make me beautiful/handsome?

BhP, because concerns regarding our looks is part of our behavior.

2. Will the teenage boys and girls like this product?

DgP, because the question is looking for the number of likes from teenage boys and girls.

3. Will the LGBTQ community be offended by these products?

BhP, because the questions is looking for the LGBTQ community reaction.

4. Will this product improve the living condition of its buyer?

DgP, because the question is looking for a percentage in living condition improvements.

5. Will consumers buy these items for their kids?​

Dgp, the question is looking for how many will the consumer buy for their kids.
