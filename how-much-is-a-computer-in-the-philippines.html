Taxable net income is the amount of income that is subject to taxation after deducting allowable expenses and exemptions. Then the tax authorities use it to determine how much income tax a taxpayer owes. Income tax due is the actual amount of tax that a taxpayer owes to the government based on their taxable net income. It is calculated by applying the applicable tax rate to the taxable net income.
