1. Is the homecoming program aimed at?​


Answer:

The back home program aims to re-socialize people who have the most diverse mental disorders and have been in hospital for a period of two years.

Thus, these people must be evaluated by a medical team that attests to their possibility of social rehabilitation, and they must be welcomed by their families and try to live a social life from that moment on.

Back home program:

This program was designed based on studies that reveal that people with mental illnesses can have quality of life while undergoing treatment, thus, together with their families, they can be more successful in this resocialization.
