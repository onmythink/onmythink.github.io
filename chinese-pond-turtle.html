2. 2. A turtle can reach the pond inof a kilometer in an hour. The turtle isof a kilometer away from a pond. At this speed, how long will it take theturtle to reach the pond?5hour c. 24 hours D D. 5 3 hours12​


Answer:

This is a D-I-R-T (Distance-Is-Rate-times-Time) problem. D=RT

The problem asks for a time. T=D/R

T = (1/2 mile) / (1/5 mile per hour)

T = (1/2)*(5/1) hours [to divide fractions, invert denominator and multiply]

T = 5/2 hours

T = 2 1/2 hours

5. What is the effect of chinese softshell turtles REACTION PAPER (minimum no. words: 300)


Answer:

The effects of whole Chinese soft-shell turtle egg powder (WTE) on immune functions is unknown. Female ICR 6-week-old mice (n=80) were administered for 30 days with 1.125 g·kg−1 WTE. Spleen and thymus were weighed and spleen lymphocyte proliferation response to concanavalin A (Con A), plaque forming cell(PFC) assay, phagocytosis assay and NK cell activity detection were performed on day 30. Compared to vehicle control, WTE increased the relative weight of spleen (p<0.05). Mice treated with WTE showed increased Con A stimulated lymphocyte proliferation (p<0.05) and PFC (p<0.01). Carbon clearance capacity (p<0.05) and NK cell activity (p<0.01) were also enhanced while a mild improvement was seen in phagocytosis capacity of peritoneal cavity macrophages in WTE treated mice. In conclusion, our data provides evidence of the enhanced effects of WTE in cell-mediated immunity, humoral immunity and NK cell activity in mice.

Mga Hayop na Ligaw at mga Endangered Species

Sa ating bansa, maraming mga hayop na maaaring maipagmalaki. Pero sa paglipas ng panahon, ang ilan sa kanila ay mga tinatawag na endangered species. Bakit? Dahil kaunti lang sila, nagbabago ang klima at nawawasak na ng tuluyan ang mga tirahan nila. Kaya ang ilan ay namamatay na.

Pamamaraan ng pag-aalaga o pagkalinga sa mga endangered na hayop:Philippines Eagle- Hindi paghuhuli sa mga ito at papatayin, kasabay nito hindi rin pagpuputol ng puno dahil nagsisilbing tirahan nila ito.

Tarsier- Kung makakita man ako ng ligaw na tarsier, ang gagawin ko ay para maalagaan ito ay ipagbigay alam sa ahensya na namamahala sa ganitong mga hayop dahil may kasanayan sila dito.

Tamaraw- Bigyan sila ng kalayaan na mamuhay ng kanila at hindi makikialam sa ginagawa nila. Kasabay nito, hindi ko pababayaan na patayin na lamang ito at hawakan ng iba lalo na kung interesado sila dito.

Spotted Dear- Alagaan ko sila at kakalingain sa pagtulong sa kanila kung may sakit o kaya naman pagalingin ang sugat nila. Maaari ko rin ito ipagbigay alam sa mga nanunungkulan may kinalaman sa pag-iingat at pag-aalaga ng ganitong mga hayop para mapanatili sila.

Pond Turtle- Pag-aalaga at paglilinis ng anyong-tubig dahil dito sila naninirahan at kumakain. Hindi paghuhuli sa kanila kahit maibenta sila sa mahal na halaga.

23. philippines pond turtle discribe the following aspects of the biology of the species 1, nesting behavior 2. mating behavior 3. breeding season​


Answer:

The Philippine pond turtle, also known as the Palawan pond turtle or Siebenrock's turtle, is a freshwater turtle species native to the Philippines. Here are some aspects of its biology:

1. Nesting Behavior

- Philippine pond turtles lay eggs on land in sandy or gravelly areas near the water. Females dig a hole with their hind legs and deposit 1 to 7 eggs per clutch, covering them with soil. The incubation period is 70 to 90 days.

2. Mating Behavior

- Male Philippine pond turtles court females by bobbing their heads, touching them with their snouts, and circling them. If the female is receptive, they will mate, but males may also compete aggressively for females during the breeding season.

3. Breeding Season

- Philippine pond turtles breed during the rainy season from June to November. Water level rise in ponds or rivers provides better nesting sites and food availability. However, captive breeding programs have successfully bred them outside of the natural breeding season.
