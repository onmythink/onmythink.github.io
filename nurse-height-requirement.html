2. Each nurse station at the hospital requires 8 nurses every shift schedule. If there are 3 shifts in one day, How many nurses are required to be at the hospital each shift if it has a total of 48 nurses stations?​


Answer:

1,152 nurses are required to be at the hospital each shift for one day.

384 nurses are required to be at the hospital for every shift schedule.

Explanation:

1 nurses station is to 24 nurses (8 nurses every shift multiplied by 3 shift in one day is equal to 24).

Thus, if there are 48 nurses stations with 24 nurses each in a one day

48×24=1,152

Then, 1,152 is the required nurses to be at the hospital each shift in one day.

But if the question just ask for the nurses to be at the hospital each shift schedule, then the answer will be 384.

1 nurses station is equal to 8 nurses every shift schedule. Thus, 48 nurses stations with 8 nurses every shift will have 384.

48×8=384.
