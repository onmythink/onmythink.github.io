23. how do plants animals and other organisms keep the biotic community alive​


Answer:

It is in a cycle. plants use nutrients and energy(sunlight) to form food. Herbivores eat plants, thus plants keep herbivores alive. Other carnivores and omnivores eat plants or other animals so they keep the next organism alive. Finally when these organisms die, fungi or bacteria (decomposers) decomposes the dead bodies of organisms and transfer the nutrients back into the environment which are for the use of Plants. So in this way each organism keep the other one alive.
