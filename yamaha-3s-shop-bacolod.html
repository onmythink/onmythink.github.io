13. solve the equation below please 3s+3s=12


Final Answer: s = 2

Solution:

Hi there! The whole idea of solving an equation is finding the unknown by isolating it, step-by-step.

In our case, we should first combine the like terms: 3s + 3s = 12

3s + 3s is just 6s.

Our equation becomes 6s = 12

Next, we divide both sides by 6. 6s/6=12/6

Simplify. s = 2

The answer to this problem is s = 2.
