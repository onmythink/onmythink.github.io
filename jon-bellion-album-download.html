He will also give a backstage pass to each eighth person. Which person will be the first to receive the free album download and a backstage pass? Explain the process you used to determine your answer.


Find the least common multiple (LCM):

6 = 6, 12, 18, 24

8 = 8, 16, 24

ANSWER: The first person to receive both the free album download and backstage pass is the 24th in line.

[ Question ]

Jon is x years old, and Nathan is twice as Jon's old.

[ Answer ]

Answer: x²

Base on the problem, Nathan is 'twice' as Jon's old. So that means, we need to apply multiplication. But we can't just do "x×2". We need to do it in exponent form. So the answer is x² or we can read it as "x is raise to the power of two". In short x² is another way of multiplying x and 2.
