Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Daniel runs 15km a day for 5 days.

In 5 days, he would cover 75 kilometers in the 5-day training.

Convert 75 kilometers to meters:

1 km = 1,000 m

75 km * 1000m = 75,000m
