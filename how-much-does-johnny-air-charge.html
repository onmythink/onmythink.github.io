22. If Johnny push the table with a Force of 200 N and it move by a distance of 10 meters, how much is the work done?


Work is Force time distance (W=F×d)

W = 200 N ×  10 m

   = 2000 J

The work done is equal to 2000 J

Problem:

If Johnny push the table with a Force of 200 N and it move by a distance of 10 meters, how much is the work done?

Given Data:

F ( Force ) = 200 N or 200 kg·m/s²

d ( distance ) = 10 m

W ( Work ) = ? unknown

Solution:

W = F × d

   = 200 kg·m/s² × 10 m

   = 2000 kg·m²/s²

   = 2000 J

Answer:

2000 J or  2000 kg·m²/s²
