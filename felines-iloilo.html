19. grouping the feline family​


Answer: The feline family, also known as Felidae, can be grouped into two main categories: big cats and small cats.

Explanation:

Big cats, which belong to the genus Panthera, include:

Lion (Panthera leo)Tiger (Panthera tigris)Jaguar (Panthera onca)Leopard (Panthera pardus)Snow Leopard (Panthera uncia)

Small cats, which belong to various genera, include:

Domestic Cat (Felis catus)Wildcat (Felis silvestris)Bobcat (Lynx rufus)Ocelot (Leopardus pardalis)Serval (Leptailurus serval)Lynx (Lynx lynx)Caracal (Caracal caracal)Cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus)

There are other species of felines, including the Puma (Puma concolor), also known as the Cougar or Mountain Lion, which are not classified as big cats because they do not belong to the Panthera genus.
