1. this festival is held on the third sunday of january in the city of cebu to honor the santo niño the petitioner often yell out pit senior​


Answer:

Sinulog Festival

Explanation:

is a traditional celebration in Cebu City held every third Sunday of January to honor the Santo Niño (Child Jesus). Basically, the festival is done by a dance ritual, in which it tells the story of the Filipino people's pagan past and their acceptance of Christianity.

People yell "Pit Senyor," a short way of saying Viva Pit Senyor, which means "Long Live the Christ Child!

30. a gargabe pit is 5 m wide and 2m deep what is the volume of the pit

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how to find volume of any 3-dimensional figure, first find the length, width and height.

Since this "garbage pit" is 5x5x2m pit, multiply the length width and height.

5 x 5 = 25

25 x 2 = 50

The volume is measured out as: centimeters cubed, meters cubes, millimeters cubed, etc.
