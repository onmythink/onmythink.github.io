What is Infographics?

Infographics (a truncated compound of "information" and "graphics") are graphic visual representations of information, facts, or knowledge that are designed to convey information quickly and clearly. They can increase cognition by enhancing the human visual system's ability to see patterns and trends with visuals. Information visualization, data visualization, statistical graphics, information design, and information architecture are all similar activities. Infographics have evolved in recent years to be used for mass communication, and as a result, they are designed with less assumptions about the knowledge base of the audience than other sorts of visualizations.
