Answer:

22. Nature Pate of Celebration Characteristics / Place of celebration Panagbenga Masskara Kaamulan Pariyar Carabao​


Explanation:

PANAGBENGA:

CHARACTERISTICS:

The festival includes floats that are covered mostly with flowers not unlike those used in Pasadena's Rose Parade. The festival also includes street dancing, presented by dancers clad in flower-inspired costumes, that is inspired by the Bendian, an Ibaloi dance of celebration that came from the Cordilleras.

PLACE OF CELEBRATION:

Baguio City, Philippines

MASSKARA:

CHARACTERISTICS:

The main feature of the Masskara Festival is the street dancing festivity in which groups of dancers wearing happy masks and colorful costumes dance and gyrate along the city streets to Latin music beat. The term “Masskara was thought of by Ely Santiago, an artist.

PLACE OF CELEBRATION:

Bacolod

KAAMULAN:

CHARACTERISTICS:

“Kaamulan” is from the Binukid word “amul” which means “to gather”. It is a gathering of Bukidnon tribespeople for a purpose. It can mean a datuship ritual, a wedding ceremony, a thanksgiving festival during harvest time, a peace pact, or all of these put together.

PLACE OF CELEBRATION:

Malaybalay City, Bukidnon

PAHIYAS

CHARACTERISTICS:

Known as “Pahiyas”, the festival is deeply rooted in the traditional celebration of the townsfolk in thanksgiving for bountiful harvest. Decorations called “Kiping”, leaf-shaped and multi-colored rice paste wafers which are used to decorate the facades of the homes along with the fruits and flowers from nature.

PLACE OF CELEBRATION:

LUCBAN QUEZON

CARABAO

CHARACTERISTICS:

A carabao race is held, and at the finish line, the animals kneel while the parish priest blesses them. The festival is also marked by exploding firecrackers and the performance of the Bamboo Dance, where dancers represent the tinikling bird, a menace to the rice crop.

PLACE OF CELEBRATION:

Pulilan Bulacan

30. it is condition originating in the perinatal period.​


Answer:

Some of the conditions are: HIV, HPV, Malaria, Chickenpox and Syphilis.

Explanation:

Conditions originating in the perinatal period are diseases that the fetus can acquire in the period from "week 28" of the gestation process until the seventh day of life outside the uterus. These perinatal diseases belong to a multifactorial clinical syndrome that involves the inheritance of vascular disease, vaginal microbial flora and nutritional status.
