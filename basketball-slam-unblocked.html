Most of my poetry was lost through years of running, using and abusing - it was burned up, snorted up or shot up.

I probably had a verse for every hit I took at some point. With addiction, it doesn't matter what or how much you have when you get clean - it can all disappear in an instant. Flat screen TV's, fancy cars, tropical vacations - I can cook each one of those material things up in a spoon and shoot it in heroin form.

But everything happens for a reason, so I give what I have and I'll keep adding more.
