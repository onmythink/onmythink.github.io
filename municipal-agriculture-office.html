28. ano ang e-wastehazardous wastebuilding wasteagricultural wastemunicipal wasteinsecticidepersticideillegal loggingmaterial recoveryfacilityreforestation ​


Answer:

1. Electronic waste or e - waste describes discarded electrical or electronic devices . Used electronics which are destined for refurbishment , reuse , resale , salvage recycling through material recovery , or disposal are also considered e - waste .

2.Hazardous waste is waste that has substantial or potential threats to public health or the environment . Hazardous waste is a type of dangerous goods .

3.Construction waste or debris is any kind of debris from the construction process . Different government agencies have clear definitions .

4. Agricultural waste refers to waste produced from agricultural operations, including waste from farms, poultry houses, and slaughterhouses.

5.Municipal waste is defined as waste collected and treated by or for municipalities.

6. Insecticides are substances used to kill insects . They include ovicides and larvicides used against insect eggs and larvae , respectively . Insecticides are used in agriculture , medicine , industry and by consumers .

7. Pesticides are substances that are meant to control pests . This includes herbicide , insecticide , nematicide , molluscicide , piscicide , avicide , rodenticide , bactericide , insect repellent , animal repellent , microbicide , fungicide , and lampricide .

8. Illegal logging is the harvest , transportation purchase or sale of timber in violation of laws . The harvesting procedure itself may be illegal , including using corrupt means to gain access to forests ; ...

9. A materials recovery facility , materials reclamation facility , materials recycling facility or Multi re - use facility is a specialized plant that receives , separates and prepares recyclable materials for marketing to end - user manufacturers .

10. Reforestation is the natural or intentional restocking of existing forests and woodlands that have been depleted , usually through deforestation , but also after clearcutting .
