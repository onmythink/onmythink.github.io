6. The super motorbike race is 540 miles long. Jonas completed 2/5 of the race while David complete 1/5 of the race. What part of the race they have completed?


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving word problems involving fraction.

Given

Motorbike race is 540 miles long.

Jonas completed 2/5 of the race.

David complete 1/5 of the race.

Problem

What part of the race they have completed?

Solution

Solve the total distance of each person completed in a race.

Jonas → 2/5 of 540

= 2/5 × 540

= 1080/5

= 216

David → 1/5 of 540

= 1/5 × 540

= 540/5

= 108

Answer

Jonas completed 216 miles long, and David completed 108 miles long of the motorbike race.

18. ric participated in a marathon race and had finished 60% of the race. if he still had 2,000 meters to run how long was the race? ​


Answer:

The race was 5,000 meters long.

Step-by-step explanation:

The total race distance: x meters

Portion Ric had finished: 60%

Remaining distance: 40% or 0.4

Remaining distance Ric still has to run:  2,000 meters

Find the total race distance:

0.4x = 2,000

0.4x/0.4 = 2,000

x = 2,000/0.4

x = 20,000/4

x = 5,000 meters

The race was 5,000 meters long.

22. cars that are racing in a race trackis this a example of kinetic energy or not​


Answer:

Yes

Explanation:

A race car, before the command to start the engine, is in potential energy. Race cars are moved to position for the race by pit crews pushing and pulling the car. The kinetic energy to move the car is coming from the crews – not the car itself. But once the command is given, the kinetic energy gets moving.
