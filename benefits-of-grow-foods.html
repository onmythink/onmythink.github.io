Answer:

1.Mangroves are the only trees that are capable of thriving in salt water. They form unique intertidal forests at the edge of land and sea, see Fig. They are represented on all continents with tropical and subtropical coasts, i.e. North and South America, Africa and Middle-East, Asia and Oceania

2.Requirements for the development of mangroves are:

*Average temperatures of the coldest month higher than 20°C; the seasonal temperature range should not exceed 5°C. ...

*A fine-grained substrate. ...

*Shores must be free of strong wave action and strong tidal currents.
