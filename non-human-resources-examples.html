2. examples of human resources and non-human resources


Answer:

Human resources are those that are less tangible. Examples of these are a person's skills, talent, personality, and knowledge. Any characteristic that can be used for productive purposes can be identified as a resource.

Non-human resources on the other hand, may also be referred to as material resources. Examples of these are time, money, goods, services, facilities, etc. Generally, these are much easier to quantify than Human Resources.
