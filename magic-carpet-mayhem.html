1. How does a book become a magic carpet or a refuge?​


Answer:

They first appeared before the 8th century in Jewish folklore, passed to Muslims after the Arab conquest. Specifically one is owned by King Solomon.

“When God appointed Solomon king over every created thing, He gave him a large carpet sixty miles long and sixty miles wide, made of green silk interwoven with pure gold, and ornamented with figured decorations. Surrounded by his four princes, Asaph ben Berechiah, prince of men, Ramirat, prince of the demons, a lion, prince of beasts, and an eagle, prince of birds, when Solomon sat upon the carpet he was caught up by the wind, and sailed through the air so quickly that he breakfasted at Damascus and supped in Media.”

They also appear in the compilation story 1001 nights, a series of children’s stories from across the Ottoman Empire. In one story, the eldest son of the Sultan of the Indies, Prince Hussain buys as a wedding gift a magical carpet from Vijayanagara. It is not a flying carpet but one that teleports whoever sits on them to wherever they wish instantly.

“'You have guessed right, sir,' replied the crier, 'and will own as much when you come to know that whoever sits on this piece of carpet may be transported in an instant wherever he desires to go without being stopped by any obstacle.'”

In the middle ages this story also passed up to Russia, where Baba Yaga gives the protagonist Ivan (who appears in two forms, Ivan the prince and Ivan the fool) a flying carpet after he impresses her. He goes on to get a fortune and rescue a princess using this carpet (often this is exchanged for a flying horse, which appears in another story of 1001 Nights)

A = πr² (radius is given)A = π (d/2)² (diameter is given)

16. A rectangular carpet has a perimeter of 172 inches. The length of the carpet is 34 inches more than the width. What are the dimensions of the carpet?


Width: x
Length: x + 34
Perimeter: 172 inches

Perimeter = 2(length) + 2 (width)
172 = 2 (x + 34) + 2x
172 = 2x + 68 + 2x
172 = 4x + 68
4x = 172 -68
4x/4 = 104/4
x = 26  

Width = 26 inches

Length = x + 34
            = 26 + 34
            = 60 inches

ANSWER:  The dimensions are 26 inches wide and 60 inches long.

Check:

2(60) + 2 (26) = 172
120 + 52 = 172
172 = 172
