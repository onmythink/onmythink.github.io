1. Directions: Define the following terms.1. On the Job Training2. Entry level Careers3. Electrocarfiograph Technician4. Cytotechnologist5. Public Health6. Epidemiology7. Caesarian8. Obstetrician9. Prophylaxis10. Orthodontist​


Answer:

1. On the Job Training: is a practical approach to acquiring new competencies and skills needed for a job in a real, or close to real, working environment.

2. Entry Level Careers: a type of job that typically requires minimal education, training and experience.

3. Electrocardiograph Technician: is an allied healthcare professional who uses specialized equipment to measure the heart's electrical activity.

4. Cytotechnologist: are lab professionals who evaluate patients' cell samples and are trained to notice subtle changes to accurately detect precancerous, malignant, and infectious conditions.

5. Public Health: defined as the science of protecting the safety and improving the health of communities through education, policy making and research for disease and injury prevention.

6. Epidemiology: the branch of medicine which deals with the incidence, distribution, and possible control of diseases and other factors relating to health.

7. Caesarian: a surgical procedure involving incision of the walls of the abdomen and uterus for delivery of offspring.

8. Obstetrician: a surgical procedure involving incision of the walls of the abdomen and uterus for delivery of offspring.
