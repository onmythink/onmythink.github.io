29. meaning of the word " animate" or "animation". how does it describe the qualities and capabilities of this art form?


Answer

Animate. It is a verb that means bringing something to life or making something lifeless move.

Animation. It is a noun that refers to an Art Technique of making an inanimate object seemingly alive by the illusion of movement. It uses several pictures in different poses to create an illusion of motion.

12 Characteristics (or Qualities) of AnimationTiming. It determines the number of frames to be used and how realistic the movements look like.Slow In and Slow Out. Slow In means that the action moves slower. Slow Out means that actions move faster. It is a gradual movement of an object. An example is throwing a punch.Arcs. It refers to natural and organic movements. It creates a fluid-like motion that makes it look natural. Unlike mechanical movements that move in a straight motion. For example, when you raise your hand your shoulder creates small "arc" movements.Follow Through and Overlapping. Follow Through means extra movement after the object stops. For example, a man lands after jumping and the necklace moves a little after landing. Overlapping means several parts moving at different rates. An example is the running motion of a four-legged animal.Squash and stretch. It gives the illusion of mass, weight, and gravity to motion. Exaggeration. This helps adds emotion or realism to motion because animations are shown on a flat surface. Straight Ahead and Pose to pose. Mostly used in 2d production. It helps the artist determined how the final appearance of the motion would look like. Pose to pose is the use of "keyframes" to pre-determined how the motion would move.Anticipation. It prepares the viewer's eye to see what action is about to happen. For example, when jumping the knee bends.Staging. It grabs the attention of the viewers and where to look at.Secondary Action. It means to add extra movement like when a person walks the arms swing a little. Appeal. This refers to how interesting a character to the viewers.Solid Drawings. This means giving the animation the illusion of Volume, Weight, and Balance. Making it look like it occupies space.
