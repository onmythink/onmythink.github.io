15. Directions: Identify what moist heat method of cooking is used in the following recipe: 11.Chicken Caldereta 12.Chicken Pastel 13.Chinese Steamed Chicken 14.Chicken Sinampalukan 15.Chicken Afritada


Answer:

The following moist heat methods of cooking are used in the mentioned chicken dishes:

Chicken Caldereta - This dish is typically cooked using the braising method, which involves browning the chicken pieces and then cooking them in a mixture of vegetables, tomato sauce, and broth in a covered pot over low heat until the meat is tender.

Chicken Pastel - This dish is typically cooked using the baking method, which involves layering chicken and vegetables in a baking dish and then covering them with a pastry crust before baking in the oven.

Chinese Steamed Chicken - This dish is cooked using the steaming method, which involves cooking chicken in a covered pot or steamer over boiling water until it is cooked through and tender.

Chicken Sinampalukan - This dish is typically cooked using the boiling method, which involves simmering chicken pieces in a mixture of water, onions, tomatoes, and tamarind until the meat is tender.

Chicken Afritada - This dish is typically cooked using the stewing method, which involves simmering chicken pieces in a tomato-based sauce with vegetables until the meat is tender.
