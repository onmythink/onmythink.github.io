11. Brief History of Louise marie madeline Fontaine ​


Answer:

Louise Marie Madeline Fontaine was a French actress and singer who was born in 1757 in Paris, France. She began her career in the early 1770s and quickly rose to fame for her talent and beauty. She became a popular figure in the Parisian theatre scene, performing in many productions at the Comédie-Française and other prominent theatres.

Fontaine's career reached its peak in the 1780s when she became one of the leading actresses of her time. She was known for her powerful voice and emotional performances, and was highly regarded by critics and audiences alike. She was also known for her love of fashion and was often seen wearing the latest styles.

In 1790, Fontaine retired from the stage and devoted herself to raising her children. She died in 1814 at the age of 57. Despite her short career, she left a lasting legacy as one of the most talented and beloved actresses of her time.

A jar contains 98 marbles, of which 37 are blue, 13 are red, and the rest are green. What is the ratio of blue marbles to green marbles?


1. The ratio of stars to hearts is 4:17, which can be simplified to 4/17.

2. The ratio of hearts to circles is 22:8, which can be simplified to 11:4.

3. The ratio of stars to all shapes is 83:9 + 83 + 1, which can be simplified to 83:(93). The simplified ratio is 83:93.

4. The ratio of circles to hearts is 32:33, which can be simplified to 32/33.

5. The ratio of yellow triangles to all triangles is 8:8 + 8, which can be simplified to 8:16. The simplified ratio is 1:2.

6. The ratio of blue marbles to green marbles is 37:(98 - 37 - 13), which can be simplified to 37:48. The simplified ratio is 37:48.
