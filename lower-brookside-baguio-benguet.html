1. benguet is to rural as baguio city is to ____​


Answer:

Rural Benguet and Urban Baguio

Benguet is to rural as Baguio City is to urban. This is because Benguet is a province, with low population density, while Baguio City is a highly urbanized city, with high population density.

Explanation:

Benguet and Baguio City are two different places. When we say Benguet, we are referring to the whole province which encompasses Atok, Bakun, Bokod, Buguias, Itogon, Kabayan, Kapangan, Kibungan, Mankayan, Sablan, Tuba, and Tublay, with La Trinidad serving as the provincial capital. When we talk about Baguio City, this is the city found within the bounds of the province of Benguet, but the administration is independent from the provincial government. This city is home to more than 300,000 people.
