Problem 3:

A large bottle of juice costs P8.30. A small bottle of juice costs P5.70. Robert buys one large bottle of juice each school day. If Robert buys small bottles of juice instead of large ones, how much money will he save each week?

Given:

large bottle of juice costs P8.30

small bottle of juice costs P5.70

school day is 5 days a week

Solution:

total costs for large bottle of juice = 5 x 8.3 = 41.5

total costs for small bottle of juice = 5 x 5.7 = 28.5

41.5 - 28.5 = 13

Answer:

He will save 13 php if he buy small bottle of juice instead of large bottle of juice.

Problem 4:

Mane is buying materials for her school portfolio. The store is selling supplies such as clear book for 149.25, a pack of colored papers for P126.75, and accessories (such as seguins) for P50.00. What would be the total if she buys 1 clear book, 2 packs of colored paper and accessories? How much would be her change if she pays P500.00 at the cashier?

Given:

clear book for 149.25 php

pack of colored papers for P126.75

accessories (such as seguins) for P50.00

Solution:

What would be the total if she buys 1 clear book, 2 packs of colored paper and accessories?

1 clear book x 149.25 = 149.25 php

2 x 126.75 = 253.5 php

total = 149.25 + 253.5

total = 402.75 php → answer

How much would be her change if she pays P500.00 at the cashier?

= 500 - 402.75

= 97.25 php → answer
