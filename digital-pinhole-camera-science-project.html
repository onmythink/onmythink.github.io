23. advantages and disadvantages of the pinhole camera​


Answer:

You can only use a very small hole. The "focal length" of a pinhole camera is the distance from the hole to where the film is. The disadvantage is the pictures are out of focus and the exposure is a hit-or-miss thing... Pinhole cameras are good for experimentation, but you REALLY need access to a darkroom.
