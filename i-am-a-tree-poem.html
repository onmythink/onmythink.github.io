BIO-POEM ABOUT NATURE

Explanation:

I am a bird in the sky. I am able to fly as high as my limit and go to any place I wish to. I give additional beauty to the gift of nature we all live in. But the beauty I loved to go back and forth before is starting to grow old as I do. And it makes me sad. Could you take care of these places for me please?

Although I'd like to think that this is a poem and also I believe its purpose was to serve as an eye opener on what's happening with our nature these days. I tried to incorporate the elements of poem as much as possible. In this case, I think this will be a freestyle one and I’d like to give emphasis on how I chose a bird as the subject of the poem.

For me, the bird symbolizes an old person who has seen the beauty of this world - untouched. And on his verge of death or as his journey comes to an end, he witnessed the drastic changes of how bad our world turned out to be and he expresses his regret that not all of us will be able to see the beauty he saw.
