9. Inverse Contrapositive dae​


Answer:

The contrapositive of the conditional statement is “If not Q then not P.” The inverse of the conditional statement is “If not P then not Q.”

Step-by-step explanation:

What is the example of converse inverse and contrapositive?

What is the example of converse inverse and contrapositive?Our converse statement would be: “If the grass is wet, then it is raining.” Our inverse statement would be: “If it is NOT raining, then the grass is NOT wet.” Our contrapositive statement would be: “If the grass is NOT wet, then it is NOT raining.”
