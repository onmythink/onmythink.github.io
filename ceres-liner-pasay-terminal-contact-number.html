8. there are 112 passengers in the first ceres bus, 118 passengers and the second, and 124 for passengers in the third and so on in an arithmetic sequence. what is the total number of passengers in the first 8 ceres bus?A.154B.910C.1064D.1224​


Answer:

C.

Step-by-step explanation:

given:

a1=112

a2=118

n=8

find: d, an and Sn

d=a2-a1

=118-112

d=6

an=a1+(n-1)d

=112+(8-1)(6)

=112+(7)(6)

=112+42

an=154

Sn=n(a1+an)/2

=8(112+154)/2

=8(266)/2

=2128/2

Sn=1064
