[tex]\underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM:}}[/tex]

Aling Nena prepares 10 different breakfast meals. In how many ways can she select 4 breakfast meals for the menu?

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER:}}[/tex]

[tex] \qquad\Large\rm» \:\: \green{C. \: 210} [/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

» Using the Combination Formula to find the number of ways in which 4 breakfast meals are selected out of 10 of them.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & \bold{\color{lightblue}Formula:} \\ & \boxed{\: \rm _nC_r = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!} \:} \end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned}\rm _{10}C_4 = \frac{10!}{4!(10 - 4)!}\end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned}\rm _{10}C_4 = \frac{10!}{4! \ 6!}\end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned}\rm _{10}C_4 = \frac{10 \cdot 9 \cdot 8 \cdot 7 \cdot \cancel{6!}}{4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot \cancel{6!}}\end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned}\rm _{10}C_4 = \frac{10 \cdot \cancel9^{ \: 3} \cdot \cancel8 \cdot 7}{ \cancel4 \cdot \cancel3 \cdot \cancel2}\end{aligned} [/tex]

[tex] \rm _{10}C_4 = 10 \cdot 3 \cdot 7[/tex]

[tex] \rm _{10}C_4 = 210[/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] There are 210 ways to choose 4 breakfast meals from 10 breakfast meals.
