17. what are the costume and tradition on new year in the philippines​


Explanation:

Media Noche.

Wearing polka dots.

Jumping high at 12 midnight.

Making loud noises. A lot of it.

Preparing 12 round fruits for good luck.

Eating pancit for long life.

Eating sticky rice cakes.

Preparing whole pork.

Wear polka dots dress. For Filipinos, wearing anything round signifies prosperity. The polka dots epitomise money and fortune

Many Filipino people unite on New Year's Eve, which is on December 31, to celebrate a midnight meal known as the Media Noche. It is also common to stay awake to greet the coming of the New Year. New Year's Day is also characterized by Filipinos lighting fireworks and making a lot of noise to drive away evil spirits.
