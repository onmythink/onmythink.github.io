9. differentiate freezing and chilling?​


Answer:

Freezing is making something turn to ice while chilling is just letting something get cold

Explanation:

Answer:

The main difference between chilling and freezing is that of temperature. Chilling refers to subjecting food products to low temperature while freezing refers to subjecting food products to temperature below their freezing point. The temperature depends on the food products. It differs from product to product.

Ice crystals are formed while freezing. While that's not in the case of chilling. Where freezing constitutes to long term preservation of food products, chilling is just for short term preservation.

Pressure from parents can have both positive and negative effects on a student's academic performance. However, too much pressure can also lead to anxiety, stress, and a lack of interest in learning, which can have a negative impact on their academic performance.
