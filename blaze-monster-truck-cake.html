30. Two (2) monster trucks are on a collision course in a monster truck event. Truck A is moving 15 m/s E with respect to the ground, while Truck B is moving 18 m/s W relative to the ground. What is the velocity of Truck B relative to Truck A?​


Let vA = 15m/s be the velocity of truck A, and
vB = 18m/s be the velocity of truck B with respect to ground. According to the velocity-addition formula, their relative velocity is:

uA = vA + vB = 33 m/s

in W direction.
