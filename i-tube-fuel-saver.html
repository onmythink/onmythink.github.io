16. WHAT ARE THE 7 HABITS OF A WISE SAVER?


Here are seven habits of a wise saver

 

1) A wise saver works hard to earn money.

2) The wise saver practices frugality, he refuses to buy on impulse and may purchase something important after a week of thinking hard.

3) They invest their money in one way or another. They make their money work for them.

4) They usually live a simple life. They are not out to impress other people with the material things they have acquired.  

5) They make sure all debts are paid.  

6) They would rather pay with cash, the thought of owing somebody money makes them uneasy.

7) They continually think of how to acquire more money and appears to have little interest for other things since money is the only reason for existing.  

The title of being a wise saver is really a blanket term when referring to a person that does not spend very much. It hides the fact that the individual may be a Scrooge, a miser, or even just plain greedy. It depends on the person but they are generally comfortable with being secure financially and won’t have it any other way. A good person who happens to be a wise saver won’t think twice about helping out when someone he knows is in dire need of financial assistance.
