29. There are 7 men and 9 women members of a club. How many committees of 4 can be formed having at least 3 mens? with the solution.​


Choose the men. Since one particular one must be in, the choice is which three men out of the remaining eight will join him; you can do this in 8C3 ways. For each of these selections, there are 7C3 ways in which the three women can be selected. Now 8C3 = 8x7x6/3! = 56 and 7C3 = 7x6x5/3! = 35. So there are 56x35 = 1960 ways of choosing the committee.
