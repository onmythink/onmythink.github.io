9. Bao can eat 12 chicken wings in 3 minutes. She eats the chicken wings at a constant rate how many chicken wings can Bao eat in 12 minutes?


Answer:

Bao can eat 48 chicken wings in 12 minutes.

Step-by-step explanation:

Ratio and Proportion

Directly Proportional Word Problem

The given problem above is an example of directly proportional word problem. Meaning, the items in the question are proportional to each other in a way that when one item increases, the other item increases also; when one item decreases, the other item decreases also.

Let us analyze the problem

What is asked?

The number of chicken wings that Bao can eat in 12 minutes.

What are the given?

12 chicken wings in 3 minutes

12 minutes

What is the equation?

Let us identify the knowns and unknowns and represent them as ratio in fraction form.

Based on the given data, we have two items in the problem, the number of chicken wings and the minutes. We can represent our ratio as chicken wings to minutes.

Let x be the missing number.

12 chicken wings in 3 minutes = ‌12⁄3‌

how many chicken wings in 12 minutes = x/12

The equation is the one below since they are directly proportional.

12⁄3‌ = x/12

Solution:

To find the missing number in a proportion, we need to cross multiply.

12⁄3‌ = x/12

3 • x = 12 • 12

3x = 144

3x/3 = 144/3

x = 48

(This symbol • is the same as the multiplication sign.)

Final Answer:

Bao can eat 48 chicken wings in 12 minutes.

Checking:

Let us check if the answer is correct. Cross multiply.

‌12⁄3‌ = ‌48⁄12‌

3 • 48 = 12 • 12

144 = 144 ✔
