Answer:

56

Step-by-step explanation:

Given that Anna can plant 56 okra seedlings in a pot and she uses 15 pots in a day, then she can plant a total of 56 x 15 = 840 okra seeds in a day.

It is also given that Maeh can plant 98 okra seedlings in a pot and she uses 8 pots in a day, then she can plant a total of 98 x 8 = 784 okra seedlings in a day.

Thus, 840 - 784 = 56 more okra seedlings.
