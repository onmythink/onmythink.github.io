Whether or not mysticism is "true" is a matter of personal belief and can vary from individual to individual. Some people may believe that mystical experiences are a genuine way to connect with the divine, while others may view them as purely psychological phenomena.

It's also worth noting that there is no scientific evidence that proves or disproves the existence of mystical experiences or their connection to the divine. Mystical experiences are subjective and cannot be easily measured or quantified.
