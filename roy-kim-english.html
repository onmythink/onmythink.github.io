17. Rio and Kim had the same amount of money at the start. When Rio gave Kim 300 pesos to Kim. The ratio of Rio's money to Kim's is 2:3. How much money did they have at the start?


Original amount of money:

Rio: x
Kim: x

New amount of money with the ratio of 2:3.

Rio: x - 300   (gave 300 to Kim)
Kim: x + 300 (add 300 the amount from Rio)

Solution:

(x - 300) : (x + 300) = 2:3

Equate the product of means to the product of extremes:

2 (x+300) = 3 (x - 300)
2x + 600 = 3x - 900
3x - 2x = 600 + 900
x = 1,500

ANSWER: The original amount of money each has is 1,500 pesos.

Check:

Rio's money to Kim's money = 2:3

(1,500-300) / (1,500 + 300) = 2/3
1,200 / 1,800 = 2/3

Divide the numerator 1,200 and denominator 1,800 by their GCF 600 to simplify to the lowest term:

(1,200/600) / (1,800/600) = 2/3
2/3 = 2/3  (True)

28. 3.The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12. Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy and Roy is 6years older than Bong. How old is Boyet?​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving linear equation.

The problem is an application of linear equation having three unknown variables.

Let

x is age of Roy

y is age of Boyet

z is age of Bong

Solution

Create three equation to solve three unknown variables.

First Equation

The sum of The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12.

x + y + z = 12

Second Equation

Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy.

y = x - 3

Third Equation

Roy is 6 years older than Bong.

x = z + 6

Solution

x + y + z = 12

y = x - 3

x = z + 6

y = x - 3 → y = (z + 6) - 3

x + y + z = 12 → (z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

Simplify

(z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

z + 6 + z + 3 + z = 12

3z + 9 = 12

Solve for z

3z + 9 = 12

3z = 12-9

3z = 3

z = 1

z = 1

x = z+6

x = 1+6

x = 7

y = x-3

y = 7-3

y = 4

Check:

x+y+z= 12

7+4+1 = 12 ✓

Answer: 4 years old
