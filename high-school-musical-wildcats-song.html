13. how many ways can you introduce the five starting players of the wildcats basketball team in the basketball game?​


Answer:

5! = 120 ways

There are five possible ways to announce the first player. For each of those, there are four possible ways to announce the second player. For each of those, there are three possible ways to announce the third player, and so on. We can count this by multiplying the different numbers of ways, getting the total as

5x4x3x2x1 = 5 = 120
