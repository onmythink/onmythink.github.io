Ingredients:

1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt

1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 teaspoon onion powder

Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

In a medium bowl, combine the shredded chicken breast, mayonnaise, search for a good yogurt for sandwich, and mustard.

Mix well until all ingredients are well combined.

Add garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.

Mix again until all ingredients are fully incorporated.

Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to use.

Use as a filling for sandwiches or wraps.
