1. The diameter of a flying disc is 10 inches. Find the circumference of the disc


CIRCUMFERENCE

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Problem:} [/tex]

The diameter of a flying disc is 10 inches. Find the circumference of the disc

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Answer:}[/tex]

[tex]\qquad \qquad \huge \rm C = 31.4[/tex]

==================================

[tex]\large\sf\underline{Solution:}[/tex]

We're given a diameter of a flying disc, 10 inches. we know that we can get the diameter of a circle by multiplying the radius by 2,therefore,plug the values into the formula C = πd.your equation becomes C = ( 3.14 )( 10 ), which simplifies to 31.4

[tex] \tiny\begin{array}{ c | c } \\ \bold{ \: Circumference = 2πr }& \bold{\: Circumference = 2\cdot 5(π) } \\ \bold{\: Circumference = (10)π} & \bold{\: Circumference = \boxed{ 31.4}} \\ \end{array}[/tex]

==================================

Answer:

968 cm or 9.68 m

Step-by-step explanation:

the string of the kite forms a right triangle with kiko and mark

use pythagorean theorem

c² = a² + b2

a²=c²-b²

a²=10²-6²

a²=100-36

a=√64

a=8 meters

total height of kite = 8m + 168cm

h = 800cm + 168cm

h = 968 cm or 9.68 m
