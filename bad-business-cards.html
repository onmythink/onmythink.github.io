Title of the story: "Yam and His Brother Sam"
Setting: March 30, at home and during the pandemic
Characters: Yam, Sam, Mommy, Daddy, Lola (Grandmother)
Plot:
A. Exposition: Sam is born, and Yam is excited to have a baby brother.
B. Rising action: Yam becomes jealous of the attention Sam receives and neglects his studies.
C. Climax: Yam has a nightmare that a big blue man is about to eat Sam, which makes him realize the importance of loving and protecting his brother.
D. Falling action: Yam promises to take care of Sam, and he starts to do better in his studies.
E. Resolution: Yam learns to love and appreciate his baby brother, and they live happily together as a family.
Reactions/Feedbacks: The story conveys an important message about the value of family and sibling relationships, as well as the dangers of excessive gadget use. It teaches readers the importance of love, care, and responsibility towards family members, especially during challenging times. The story can serve as a great example to encourage children to build healthy sibling relationships and to prioritize their studies over technology.
