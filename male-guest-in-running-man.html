Answer: Each male guest eat 4 hotdogs.

Step-by-step explanation:

There are 18 female guests and including Nina,

19 (girls) × 3 hotdogs = 57 hotdogs

16 (boys) × 4 hotdogs = 64 hotdogs

57 hotdogs + 64 hotdogs = 121 hotdogs
