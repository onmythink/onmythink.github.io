Problem:

A 60 kilogram tourist tries horseback riding in Tagaytay. The horse has a mass of 500 kilogram.He moves around a circular path with a radius of 10 meter at a rate of 5 meter per second.Compute the centripetal force.

Given Data:

m ( mass ) = 60 kg + 500 kg = 560 kg

r ( radius ) = 10 m

v ( velocity ) = 5 m/s

Fc (centripetal force) = ? unknown

Solution:

We can solve the problem using the formula Fc = mv²/r

Fc = mv²/r  

  = 560 kg × (5 m/s)² / 10 m

  = 560 kg × 25 m²/s²  /  10 m

  = 14,000 kg·m²/s² / 10 m

  = 1,400  kg·m/s² or  1,400 N

Answer:

the centripetal force  is 1,400  kg·m/s² or  1,400 N
