21. sonya is eight years older than marites if the sum of their ages is 42 , how old is marites​


Answer:

Marites is 34 year old.

Step-by-step explanation:

42 - 8 = 34

Sonya is eight years older than Marites. If the sum of their ages is 42, how old is Marites​?

ㅤ

Marites is 17 years old

ㅤ

Let [tex]M[/tex] be Marites' age then Sonya's age will be [tex]M+8.[/tex] If the sum of their ages is 42, we have the equation:

[tex]M+(M+8)=42[/tex]

Solving,

[tex]2M+8=42[/tex]

[tex]2M+8-8=42-8[/tex]

[tex]2M=34[/tex]

[tex]\dfrac{2M}{2}=\dfrac{34}{2}[/tex]

[tex]\therefore M=17[/tex]

Thus, Marites is 17 years old.
