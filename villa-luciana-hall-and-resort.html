5. luciana give 1/4 of the cake to one friend and 1/3 to another friend how much of the cake is left if she started with the whole cake​


Answer:

[tex] \frac{5}{12} [/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

Add 1/4 and 1/3 to get the total portions of the cake given:

[tex] \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{3 + 4}{12} = \frac{7}{12} [/tex]

to get the portions of the cake left, subtract it with the whole cake (1):

[tex]1 - \frac{7}{12} = \frac{12 - 7}{12} = \frac{5}{12} [/tex]

to check:

[tex] \frac{7}{12} + \frac{5}{12} = \frac{12}{12} = 1[/tex]

The hotel features its own underwater coral structures that support a marine eco-system.
