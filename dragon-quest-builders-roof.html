7. All the little dragons on the high-tilted eaves of the green roofs (3) shiver to the tips of their gilded tails under that deep wave of sound. The underlined word means to _____. a. fall on one’s knees b. tremble at the loud sound c. be destroyed by the gilded tails


Answer:

The correct answer is  B.

Explanation:

Cambridge Dictionary defines shivering as "the act of shaking slightly" due to cold, fear, or illness. Likewise, tremble has a similar definition.

Although shiver could also mean shatter or being broken, the way it used in this sentence is more appropriately describing the trembling of the little dragons to the tips of their tails caused by the deep wave of sound they heard. Furthermore, there is no act of violence described in the sentence to cause destruction to their gilded tails.

Other terms for shiver include: quiver, shake, vibrate, flutter, shudder, etc.
