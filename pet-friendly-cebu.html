3. make a project plan entitled "PET-FRIENDLY TOWEL"​


Answer:

Project title: "PET-FRIENDLY TOWEL"​

Objective:

Able to make a pet-Friendly towel

Materials and Tools:

Microfiber clothFabric glueSewing kit (tools etc.)Extra fabric

Material Quantity, Unit Cost

Mic ro fiber cloth: 20x19cm, 65pesos

Fabric Glue: 1pcs, 35pesos

Extra fabric: 45 pesos

Total: 145 pesos

tools: Iron. sewing machine

Procedure​;

Cut a length of fabric that is three times the width that you want the finished trim to be and a little longer than the width of your towel. I do 3 inchesthen folded it in half with the wrong sides facing and ironed a crease into the middle of the strip. Then, to turn it into a bias binding, I turned 1.5 inches over at the top of each side and ironed these edges too.placed the edge crease 3 inches from the hem of the towel and pinned the binding strip to the towel with the wrong side facing up. I straight stitched with a sewing matching along the crease.I then folded the binding strip over the edge of the towel so that the right sides are out and pinned the folded edge crease to the towel before sewing along this edge with a straight stitch to seal the binding.At the ends of the strip, I folded the excess under the binding and then hand-stitched the opening closedrepeat these steps for the second towel and after only an hour and a half, I had ‘new’ custom-made towels that matched my blind and fitted in with my new seaside bathroom theme perfectly!

11. the packaging is environment friendly way or not?explain the friendly way and not​


Answer:

Environment friendly packaging refers to packaging materials and designs that have minimal impact on the environment. There are two types of packaging: eco-friendly and non-eco-friendly.

Eco-friendly packaging - this is designed with an intention to minimize the negative impact to the earth. This may include using biodegradable materials like corn starch, recycled paper, or reusable alternatives like glass, metal, and canvas. Such packaging can be recycled and do not release harmful chemicals to the environment.

Non-eco-friendly packaging - This type of packaging has some negative impact on the environment. It includes materials that release harmful gasses, require huge quantities of energy and water during production, and not easy to reuse or recycle.

Ultimately, it is essential to make use of eco-friendly packaging because it helps to reduce the carbon footprint and environmental impact. Businesses can make sustainable choices in their packaging materials to create a positive impact on the planet.
