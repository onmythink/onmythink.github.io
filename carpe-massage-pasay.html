5. There are 725 carps and some goldfish in an aquarium. There are five times as many carps as goldfish. How many fishes are there in the aquarium?


Answer:

Let's call the number of goldfish "x."

According to the problem, there are five times as many carps as goldfish. So the number of carps is 5x.

We also know that the total number of fish is 725.

Putting this all together, we can write the equation:

x + 5x = 725

Simplifying, we get:

6x = 725

Dividing both sides by 6, we get:

x = 120.83

Since we can't have a fraction of a fish, we need to round this down to the nearest whole number. So there are 120 goldfish.

To find the number of carps, we can use the equation we came up with earlier:

carps = 5x

carps = 5(120)

carps = 600

So there are a total of 120 + 600 = 720 fish in the aquarium.

9. the princess and the red carp summary


Answer

The Story of "The Princess and the Red Carp" or "Ye Xian" in Chinese is considered as a variant of the Cinderella Story.

Summary

Ye Xian is a daughter of a chieftain but then her father, Wu, dies from a plague. Wu didn't have any sons so a new chieftain took his place. Ye Xian's family then falls into poverty and Ye Xian became a slave/servant for her stepmother, Jin, and her half-sister, Jun-Li. Despite the abuses from her step-family, Ye Xian found peace in a lake near their home. She became friends with a 10-ft Red Carp with golden eyes and scales. It is later revealed that the Red Carp was a guardian spirit sent by Ye Xian's deceased Mom.

Eventually, Jun-Li discovered Ye Xian's secret place and told her mother. Jin then tricked Ye Xian to give her worn dress to catch the Red Carp and eat it. Devasted by the death of her beloved fish friend, a spirit of an Old Man appeared before Ye Xian. The Old Man told her to bury the bones of the fish in a pot and hide it under the corner of her bed. He also told Ye Xian that she can ask whatever she needs and the pots will grant it.

(this is where the Cinderella part begins)

A celebration of the New Year Festival is approaching. It is a festival daughters would find potential husbands. The stepmother, Jin, didn't want her daughter, Jun-Li, to miss this opportunity. So she ordered Ye Xian to stay and clean the cave-house. The spirit of the Red Carp visits Ye Xian and she makes a silent wish to the bones. Ye Xian is then transformed into a beautiful lady wearing a green silk dress and a pair of tiny golden slippers. The Carp warned her that she shouldn't lose any of her golden slippers.

Ye Xian arrives at the festival and many young males adored her beauty, for they thought she was a princess. But she feared that her stepsister will recognize her and she hastily left the festival leaving one of the golden slippers behind. The golden slippers were found by a merchant and were passed on several people until it reached the King of the To'Han islets. The king ordered a decree to find the maiden that fits into this golden slipper. However, none matched the size of the golden slippers. And so, the King made pavilion and place the slipper for display. Ye Xian tried to retrieve the slippers but was caught and brought to the King. She told everything about the Red Carp and the kind-hearted king believed and allowed her to go home with the golden slipper.

The next day, the King visited Ye Xian in her stepmother's house and offered here to be his queen. Ye Xian wore her complete dress and reveals that she is the beautiful maiden seen during the festival. However, the stepmother accuses Ye Xian that she stole the dress from her daughter. The King saw the wickedness in Jin's heart and didn't believe her words. So Ye Xian and the King left the cave-house. With the absence of Ye Xian, Jin made her daughter, Jun Li do the slavery works that Ye Xian did. Eventually, Jun Li rebelled against her mother and the two fought which caused the cave to collapse on the two.
