22. What is species nova in microbial taxonomy?


Taxonomy is a scheme of classifications. In the field of Biology, organisms and microorganisms are classified based on specific characteristics and features.

The words novus and nova translates to "new" in Latin. In biological or microbial taxonomy, a species nova directly translates to new species.

An author who inserts n.sp. or sp. nov. before a species name - usually referred to as a scientific name - thereby states the idea that the specimen is a new species or a previously undescribed species.
