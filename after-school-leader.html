First person - a set of words that refers to the person who is speaking or writing. First person refers to the speaker. we, us, our and ourselves are all first pronouns. Specifically, they are plural first person pronouns. Singular first pronouns are I, me, my, mine and myself.

Example:

1. I already buy a new shoes.

I is a singular first pronoun. It is a first person.

Third person - it belongs to the person or people being talked about. It also refers to the one that is neither the speaker or writer of the word in which they occur. The third person pronouns include he, him, his, himself, she, he, hers, herself, it, its, itself, they, their, theirs and themselves.

Example:

1. Judy used her money to buy a new house.

her is a third person.
