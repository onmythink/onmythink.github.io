Step-by-step explanation:

Since all of the given choices are in dollars, we just need to compare which of them is the least. To do that, we will look at the digits one by one starting with the highest place value. The highest place value of the three are thousands so, to make more easier let use write them aligning their digits with their corresponding place values.

3 1 9 2

5 3 0 0

7 4 5 5

Now, the digits in the thousands place are 3, 5, and 7. The least of the three is 3. Therefore, that number, 3,192, is the least among the three.
