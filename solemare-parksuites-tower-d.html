28. What is the angle of inclination formed by an observer to an object kept above a tower if the horizontal distance between the observer and the tower as well as the height of the tower are both 150 ft. A. 45 deg B. 60 deg C. 30 deg D. 90 deg


Answer:

-A

Explanation:

-The angle of inclination formed by an observer to an object kept above a tower if the horizontal distance between the observer and the tower as well as the height of the tower are both 150 ft is 45°. This can be determined by using the right triangle trigonometric ratio of tangent, where tangent of the angle of inclination is equal to the ratio of the height to the horizontal distance.
