If I were James, I would take the following steps:

Report the bed bug infestation to the hotel's housekeeping department or manager as soon as possible. This is important to prevent the spread of bed bugs to other rooms.

Advise the hotel management to call a professional pest control service to handle the bed bug infestation. It is important to have the bed bugs eliminated completely and effectively.

Isolate the room and prevent any other guests from checking in until the bed bug infestation has been completely eradicated.

If the room is to be cleaned, ensure that proper protective gear is used, such as gloves and shoe covers, to avoid spreading the bed bugs to other areas of the hotel.

Inform the hotel guests in adjacent rooms about the infestation and provide them with alternative accommodations if possible.

If the hotel management is slow to respond or does not take adequate measures to address the infestation, consider contacting local health authorities to report the issue.

It's important to take immediate action to address bed bug infestations in hotels to prevent their spread to other rooms and ensure the comfort and safety of guests.
