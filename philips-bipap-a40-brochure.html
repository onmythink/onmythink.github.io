      x + 5 - Philip's weight

Given: 91 kilos - total weights of Ronnie and Philip

Solve for Ronnie's weight.

x + (x + 5) = 91

2x + 5 = 91

2x + 5 + -5 = 91 + -5

2x = 86

2      2

x = 43 kilos

Therefore, Ronnie weighs 43 kilos.

Solve for Philip's weight.

x + 5 = 43 + 5 = 48 kilos

Therefore, Philip weighs 48 kilos.

To check:

x + (x + 5) = 91

43 + 48 = 91

91 = 91
