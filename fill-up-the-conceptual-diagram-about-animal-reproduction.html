2. fill up the conceptual diagram about animal reproduction​


The conceptual diagram about animal reproduction​.

Certain creatures create progeny through asexual reproduction, whereas others produce offspring through sexual reproduction. Both methods have advantages and disadvantages. Asexual reproduction produces genetically identical offspring since the children are all clones of the original father. Asexually reproducing creatures have two advantages over sexually reproducing species: they can produce large numbers of progeny from a single individual. In a stable or predictable environment, asexual reproduction is a successful method of reproduction since all children will be acclimated to that environment. Because all progeny are genetically identical and may not be suited to varied environments, asexually reproducing creatures may be at a disadvantage in an unstable or unpredictable environment.
