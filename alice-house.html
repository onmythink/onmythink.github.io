25. Alice and her husband planned to build a play space for their daughter Aliyah ontheir yard with a circular sector or radius 2. 25 m and a central angle of 1200. Whatis the area covered by the mini playground?ProposedAlice's houseplayground.


The area of a circular sector is equal to: (angle/360) * pi * r^2

Plugging in the values from the problem, we get: (1200/360) * pi * (2.25)^2 = 2.25 * pi * (2.25)^2 = 5.0625 * pi

The area of the circular sector is approximately 5.0625 * pi = 15.97 square meters.

This is the area covered by the mini playground. If you would like to propose a design for Alice's house playground, you will need to provide more information about the dimensions and layout of the space.
