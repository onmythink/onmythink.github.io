11. compare the time of sunrise on june 20 2020 with that on December 20 2020 on which day did the sunrise earlier​


Answer:

The sun rise earlier in June 22, 2011 (in the Philippines).  

Explanation:  

Equinox

The word equinox came from the Latin words "equi" meaning equal and "nox" meaning night.   Equinox happens when the amount of day-time is equal to the amount of night time and it is observed twice a year.  Equinox occur every time the sun's position is right above the equator.  

Solstice

There are two types of solstice, the summer and the winter solstice. The summer occurs around the month of June while the winter solstice occurs around December.

During summer solstice, longer day light or day time is experienced. On the other hand, during winter solstice,  longer night time is experienced.

Now for considering the situation of the question, according to the data, here are the time of sunrise and sunset in the Philippines for the following dates:

June 22, 2011 (Wednesday)

Sunrise: 5:28 AM

Sunset: 6:28 PM

December 22, 2011 (Thursday)

Sunrise: 6:16 AM

Sunset: 5:32 PM

From these, it is obvious that the sun has risen earlier on June 22 compared to December 22 (5:28 AM vs. 6:16 AM). But it can be seen on the data that even though June 22 has earlier sunrise, it has late sunset compared to December 22 (6:28 PM vs. 5:32 PM).

We can conclude that the day time during the second quarter of the year (April to June) where the month of June belongs to is longer than the fourth quarter of the year (October to December) where the month of December belongs to.

26. starting from the front door of your ranch house, you walk 40.0m west to a bench where you sit and watch the sunrise. it takes you 28.0 to walk from your house to the windmill and then 36.0 to walk from the windmill to the bench. for the entire trip from your front door to the bench, what are your average velocity and your average speed​


Answer:

Average velocity = -0.625 m/s
Average speed = 1.625 m/s

Explanation:

Average velocity is defined as the displacement (change in position) divided by the time taken. In this case, the displacement is the distance between the front door of the ranch house and the bench, which is 40.0 m to the west.

The time taken for the entire trip is the sum of the time taken to walk from the front door to the windmill and the time taken to walk from the windmill to the bench, which is 28.0 + 36.0 = 64.0 s.

Therefore, the average velocity is:

Average velocity = displacement / time taken

= -40.0 m / 64.0 s (since the displacement is to the west)

= -0.625 m/s

Note that the negative sign indicates the direction of the displacement (west).

Average speed is defined as the total distance traveled divided by the time taken. In this case, the total distance traveled is the sum of the distances traveled from the front door to the windmill and from the windmill to the bench, which is 28.0 + 36.0 + 40.0 = 104.0 m.

Therefore, the average speed is:

Average speed = total distance / time taken

= 104.0 m / 64.0 s

= 1.625 m/s
