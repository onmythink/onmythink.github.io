5. explain the message it had been saved by a miracle -a miracle of faith and love​


Answer:

The phrase "it had been saved by a miracle - a miracle of faith and love" refers to the ability of anything or someone to be delivered from a trying or life-threatening circumstance. The term "miracle" implies that this redemption was unanticipated or improbable and that it can be attributed to a higher power or energy. The expression "miracle of faith and love" alludes to the fact that everyone involved's belief and concern contributed to the outcome, proving the strength of these two qualities to overcome even the most formidable obstacles. Overall, the message emphasizes the value of love and faith in helping us get beyond whatever challenges we may encounter in life.
