If a/b=1/2=0.5, and the rest simplify up to 1/2 again until 130/260, you are adding 1/2 or a/b 130 times, because the pattern adds 1 to “a” every step, and since it is again supposed to be equivalent to 1/2, “a” is multiplied by 2 which is “b”, so “b=2a”. 1/2=0.5 0.5x130=65
