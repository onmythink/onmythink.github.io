17. LRT-2 train left santolan station at 8:0 a. m.and arrived at the recto station at 8:30 a.m if the distance between the two station is 13 kilometers what was the average speed of the train


To find the average speed of the train, we can use the formula:

average speed = total distance ÷ total time

In this case, the total distance is 13 kilometers (the distance between Santolan Station and Recto Station), and the total time is 30 minutes (the time it took for the train to travel from Santolan Station to Recto Station).

However, the formula for average speed requires that the distance and time are expressed in the same units. Since the distance is in kilometers and the time is in minutes, we need to convert the time to hours:

30 minutes = 30 ÷ 60 hours = 0.5 hours

Now we can substitute the values into the formula:

average speed = 13 km ÷ 0.5 hours = 26 km/h

Therefore, the average speed of the train was 26 kilometers per hour.

Note: It's important to keep in mind that this is the average speed over the entire journey from Santolan Station to Recto Station. The actual speed of the train may have varied at different points along the way depending on factors such as traffic and station stops.

A gym has 18 exercise stations, including 8 ellipticals. What is the probability that a randomly selected exercises station will be an elliptical?

The probability that an elliptical station will be selected is 8/18 or 0.44 or 44%.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

What is the probability that a randomly selected exercises station will be an elliptical?

What are given?

number of exercise stations - 18

number of elliptical stations - 8

What is the formula?

Probability = events/outcomes

In finding the probability of selecting an elliptical station, we need to know the number of possible events to happen and total number of possible outcomes.

In our problem, number of events is 8 because there are 8 elliptical stations. Then, the total number of possible outcomes is 18 because there are 18 exercises stations.

Solution:

Probability = events/outcomes

= 8/18

We can express this in decimal form=0.44 or percentage form 44%.
