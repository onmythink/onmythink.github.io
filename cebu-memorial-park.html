Ang salitang "makasaysayan" (o "makasaysayang" bilang partikular na salita mula sa iyong inihayag) ay naglalarawan naman sa pangngalang "landing".

19. Why do you think MacArthur Island Memorial National Park is important?​


Answer:

Because it commemorates the historic landing of General Douglas MacArthur in Leyte Gulf at the start of the campaign to recapture and liberate the Philippines from Japanese occupation on 20 October 1944.

- Commonly known as MacArthur Park, is one of Leyte's significant landmarks. It is where the famous bronze statue of General Douglas MacArthur is found which commemorates his historic return to the island of Leyte on the early afternoon of October 20, 1944.
