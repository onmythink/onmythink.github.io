Deciduous trees are composed of broad leaves that change color during fall and spread their seeds using flowers. Coniferous trees have needle-like leaves, color does not change, and they use cones to spread their seeds. Fruit-bearing trees produce fruit when their ripened ovaries in flowers contain one or more seeds.

11. similarities and differences of family tree ang phylogenetic tree​


Answer:

The differences of family tree ang phylogenetic tree​: Phylogenies trace patterns of shared ancestry between lineages.

The similarities of family tree ang phylogenetic tree​: The root of the tree represents the ancestral lineage, and the tips of the branches represent the descendants of that ancestor. As you move from the root to the tips, you are moving forward in time. When a lineage splits (speciation), it is represented as branching on a phylogeny.
