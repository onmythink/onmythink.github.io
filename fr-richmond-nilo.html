1. Richmond runs a distance of 100 meters in 12 seconds. How fast does Richmond runs?


Speed

Speed is a scalar quantity that describes the displacement covered by a moving object over an interval. In short, it describes how fast a moving object moved from one position to another within a certain period of time. We have two quantities to take note of - displacement and time.

The formula for speed is [tex]S=\frac{displacement}{time}[/tex].

Answer

8.33 meters/second

Problem

Richmond runs a distance of 100 meters in 12 seconds. How fast does Richmond run?

Solution

Given:

d = 100 meterst = 12 seconds

Formula: [tex]S=\frac{displacement}{time}[/tex]

Solving. Simply substitute the given values into the equation of speed.

[tex]S=\dfrac{d}{t}[/tex]

[tex]S=\dfrac{100\ m}{12\ s}[/tex]

[tex]S=\bold{\dfrac{25}{3}\ m/s}[/tex]

[tex]S=\bold{8.33\ m/s}[/tex]

∴ Richmond runs at a pace of 8.33 m/s.

Question :-

It took Ronald 6 hours to walk to Nilo's house at 2 kilometers per hour, how far is Nilo's house?

Answer :-

Nilo's house is 12 kilometers far.

[tex] \blue{ \overline{ \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad}}[/tex]

Solution :-

As per the provided information in the given question, we have been given that the speed is 2 km/h. The time is 6 hours. We have been asked to find or calculate the distance.

To calculate the distance, we will use the formula below :-

[tex]\qquad\quad\bigstar \: \: \boxed{\sf{ \: \: \: Distance = Speed \times Time \: \: \:}}[/tex]

Substitute the given values into the above formula and solve for Distance :-

[tex]\sf:\implies{Distance = Speed \times Time }[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies{Distance = 2 \: km/h \times 6 \: hours }[/tex]

[tex]\sf:\implies\bold{Distance = 12 \: km}[/tex]

Therefore :-

Nilo's house is 12 kilometers far.
