1. A 2008 kg cart moves in a rotonda at 8m/s. If the cart exerts 8N of centripetal force to the track. Find the radius of the rotonda??

Given:

Mass - 2 008 kgVelocity - 8 m/sCentripetal Force - 8 N or 8 kg.m/s²

Answer & Solution:

[tex]\large\sf r=mv^2÷Fc[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf r=2 \: 008 \: kg(8 \: m/s)^2÷8 \: kg.m/s²[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf r=2 \: 008 \: kg(64 \: m^2/s^2)÷8 \: kg.m/s²[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf r=128 \: 512 \: kg.m²/s²÷8 \: kg.m/s²[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf r=16 \: 064 \: kg.m²/s²[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf r=\boxed{\bold{\red{16 \: 064 \: m}}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\sf\therefore The \: radius \: is \: \boxed{\bold{16 \: 064 \: m}} .[/tex]
