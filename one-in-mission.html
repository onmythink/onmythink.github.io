6. how does one's baptism fulfill our mission as sons and daughters of god​


A person’s baptism fulfills their mission as sons and daughters of God. Baptism is a public affirmation that a person chooses to be God’s servant. This means that from that day forward the owner of his life is the Almighty God. He or she will do God’s will in all aspects of their life. Upholding a good conscience and good relationship with God is the most important thing in their life.

Meaning of Baptism

Water baptism in the name of God, Son, and Holy Spirit means the following for Christians:

Accepting God’s name, organization, authority, purpose, and laws.-Psalm 83:18Knowing the name, office, and authority of Jesus Christ in God’s Kingdom.- Matthew 16:16Acknowledging the purpose and action of the holy spirit in the fulfillment of God’s purpose.- John 14:16,17

Importance of Baptism

Although baptism is not a guarantee for salvation (Matthew 24:13), still it is important to be baptized because:

It is a public expression of our love to Almighty God.Living morally upright results in good conscience, fulfilling life, and inner peace.Engage in doing spiritual things like sharing the good news.Hope to receive everlasting life in the near future.
