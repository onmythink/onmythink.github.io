1. what is monotype printing​


Answer:

monotyping

Explanation:

Monotyping is a type of printmaking made by

drawing or painting on a smooth , non-absorbent surface. The Surface , or matrix

was historically a copper etching plate, but in

contemporary work it can vary from zinc or

glass to acrylic glass.
