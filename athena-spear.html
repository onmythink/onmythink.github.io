Answer:

1330

Step-by-step explanation:

x=original amount of the two

after spending:

albright: x - 1425

athena: x - 285

since after spending, athena will have 7 times albright's remaining amount, the equation will be:

7(x-1425)=x-285

x(original amount)=1615

therefore, athena's remaining amount is 1615-285 which is 1330.
