14. nicky is 3 times as old as her daughter casey. the sum of their ages is 48 years . find the age of each ​


You can solve this problem by first using a variable to represent Casey's age (x).  Now, since Casey's age is represented by x and we know that Nicky is 3 times as old as Casey, then Nicky's age is represented as 3x.  We can now use an equation to solve for their ages:

x + 3x = 48

4x = 48

x = 12

We found that x = 12, which is Casey's age and Nicky's age is 3(12) = 36.
